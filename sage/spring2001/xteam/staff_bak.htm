Meet the Staff!

Founder

Andy Wolan (wacko@vgernet.net)
Andy is the original site maintainer and also the owner of the Emulation Zone in which SSRG is hosted on. When a lot of events were going in the Sonic scene such as the Sonic 2 beta, Sonic ROM hacking, and several other findings, the Andy started the SOnic Stuff Research group which allowed yhese findings to go public and to create a new Sonic hacking based scene. Although, not the maintainer anymore, he still comes back every so often to fix up some things and to do things for SSRG.

Maintainer

Damian Grove (saxman@shentel.net)
Damian (that's me) is the current SSRG maintainer. Although, could be considered the "president" of SSRG, I still consder myself at Andy's level of ownership and managing. When Andy got a lot lesser time to work on SSRG and Emulation Zone at the same time, he desided to hand over SSRG to somebody else by vote. Surprisinly, my vote was higher, so here I am, still maintaining and making the best out of SSRG.

Spanish Mirror

Wiseman (wiseman@emux.net)
Wiseman is the guy we depend on to translate the page into Spanish. He has also done a translation of Tom's Sonic Hacking pages. This is the type of guy who will do a lot of hard work to make sure SSRG is looking good not only to the English speaking people, but also to Spanish speaking people!

Secrets of Sonic the Hedgehog

Andre Dirk (andre@emulationzone.org)
This is the guy who made the big SSRG boom with his famous site, Secrets of Sonic the Hedgehog. This site's purpose was to release many secrets in which some people didn't know about and to release the mysteries that have yet to be solved. To this day, Sonic fans of all kinds are releasing secret info. This popular routine wouldn't have happened without Andre Dirk. Although, he doesn't maintain/own the site anymore, he will never be forgotten.

Tim Myers (Knuckles) (ilovesega@hotmail.com)
Knuckles is now the new maintainer of Secrets of Sonic the Hedgehog. He is keeping the page alive after it took a fall in popularity. Now things are back on track and Secrets of Sonic the Hedgehog is even more popular than before with it's new sections and features.

Jan Abaza (bojan.abaza@guest.arnes.si)
This guy put together Area 51 before the Sonic 2 beta was out, but shotly after the beta was released to the public, this site as to many other sites got a big boom.
