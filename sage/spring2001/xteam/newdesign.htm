Sonic Stuff Research Group:..
Welcome to the Sonic Stuff research group! We are a small group of very devoted Sonic the Hedgehog fans who have decided to share our knowledge with you. This is NOT another general Sonic site, but rather an archive of information that (at least originally ) is rare, yet very interesting. We have inspired several to follow in our footsteps, but we are the original, and we are still dedicated to being the best you can find.
