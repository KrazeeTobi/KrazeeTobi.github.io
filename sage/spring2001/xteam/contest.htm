Sonic Custom Level Contest

Welcome to the "Fall 2000 - Sonic Custom Level Contest"! This is a special event we have put together for you to prove that your level is the best there is! We encourage everyone to enter. For those of you who are not fermiliar with the world of Sonic level editing, do not fear. We have prepared a page where you can get started! Just check out http://ssrg.emulationzone.org/mazes/start.html to get the info you need. We will offer a total of 4 categories. The first one is Sonic Standard Levels which will be the regular game levels from Sonic 1, 2, or 3K. The second one is Sonic 1 Special Stages, the third is Sonic 2 Special Stages, and the last one is Sonic 3K Special Stages. The contest has begun and will end November 24th (Sonic 2's birthday!). From there we will decide the ranks. However, if you don't win, don't feel bad. We will have a new contest again in the Winter, so you will always have another chance. So start submitting those levels!
