SSRG Exclusive Sonic Custom Levels CD

This project is in the works currently. This CD will offer 100 custom 'exclusive' levels for Sonic 1, 2, and 3K. These levels will not be avalible for download off the internet however. These levels will only be avalible on this CD. The CD cost is planned at $15. This is 'extremely' cheap for the amount of work put forth to make all these levels. That's where YOU come in. I am trying to get the Sonic community involved a great deal here. You will receive 15 cents for each level you submit once the CD is complete and we get the money to pay you. However, it's a long way from complete. If everyone participates though, it will happen much sooner. Once we get enough levels, we will immediatly close offerings for levels. If we don't think your level is up to correct standards that the public would generally want, don't get upset. Just fix it up -- or you could have us fix it up if you want, either way. You must however agree that any levels you submit will be for profit reasons and therefore, should not be avalible to the public outside this CD -- this includes the internet, putting them on a disk of your own, etc. Participating in making levels for this CD will not only give you your profit of 15 cents per every level you send in, but it will also get you a free copy of this CD.
