In the latest game from Genix, we continue where Sega left off. At the end of Sonic and Knuckles 3, Robotnic was lost in space, never to be seen again. Well, he survived (in our continuity anyway), and landed on the moon. From there he launches a massive attack on Mobius. The only thing that stands in his way are Sonic and Co. 

"Get to play as all your favorite characters in this massive epic, the best to come from Genix yet." And then we woke up :)
