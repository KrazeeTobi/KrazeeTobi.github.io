March 9th, 2001

        As the title says, "We are ready for S.A.G.E.!"  I must say, I am happy that we, The Sonikku Team, are happy that we could make it this time.  Last S.A.G.E. we weren't prepared, nor did we really have anything to show off or announce.  This time we have a couple of goodies for you guys!

- First off, we have a Metal Sonic Quest 2 demo!  The graphics are just awesome, as well as the game play.  We have worked very hard on this demo and we hope that you guys will be satisfied with it.  You can find the demo in the Game Downloads section of our booth, and if you're curious about the history of Metal Sonic Quest 2, check out the Game Information section of our booth.

- Second, we have a preview of Metal Sonic Quest 3 ready for you guys (in the MSQ2 demo)!  But we're not just going to hand it over..  I think we should make you guys go on a little hunt.  Within all of the sections of the booth, the password is hidden.  If you are one of the lucky ones to figure out what the password is, put the password in the MSQ2 demo.. and there you have it!

- And finally, another version of Metal Sonic Quest is planned!  As you all know, the graphics in Metal Sonic Quest: Special Edition aren't the best in the world (although, the graphics do work pretty well for the game).  We are planning to make a graphically enriched version of Metal Sonic Quest: Special Edition!  We are also planning on making some of the levels in the game longer, since a few of the levels did seem kind of short.

          And that's all we have for S.A.G.E.  We hope we satisfied you all!!

--CyberKnux, Vice President
