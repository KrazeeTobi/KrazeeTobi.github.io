Metal Sonic Quest 2:Return and Revenge, is a continuation of the story from the original Metal Sonic Quest.  The main plot line goes... It has been several months since Metal Sonic stopped Dr. Robotniks plans, and was soon deactivated himself.  But now Robotnik has decided to try using a robotic clone of Sonic to carry out and back up his latest evil plan by eliminating Sonic and his allies.  He has now created Metal Sonic 2, a similar but bigger, and more powerful version of the original Metal Sonic. However...to avoid the mistakes that were made last time, he has created a new personality blockage program and will install it within MS2 Upon activation. When activated, this program would hold back any feelings of resistance, or rebellion and fill those gaps with total loyalty, while still allowing Sonic's basic knowledge to come through....

Now your mission is to take on Sonic and the others to help out your master with his plan...however in this game...the only side you're on is your own.
