1993
The year of many Sonic games!

<<1991 | 1992 | 1993 | 1994 | 1995 | 1996 | 1997 | 1998 | 1999 | 2000 | 2001>>

This is the year where things got busy for Sonic the Hedgehog. Lots more games were released to please the Sonic-maniacs throughout the country, and then... the merchandise came out. Yes, this year was most prosperous for Sonic. Here's the games that came out this busy year...

Sega Sonic Arcade - Arcade Machines
June 1993

Although not much is known in the west about the Arcade game featuring everyone's favourite hedgehog, as the machine never saw light of day outside Japan. The game had three characters - Sonic, Ray the squirrel and Mighty the Armadillo. The characters were controlled by a large trackball built in the arcade machine, and players ventured through a world similar to Sonic 3D Blast. You can still play Sega Sonic Arcade, provided you grab the necessary ROM and Emulator for the game, although Emulation is pretty slow at this stage.

See more information on Sega Sonic Arcade - Arcade

Sonic the Hedgehog CD - Sega Mega CD
June 1993

Due to lack of space on the Sonic 2 cartridge, Sonic Team set their time travelling sights on Sonic CD, the only Sonic game to grace the Mega CD, and possibly the only good game on it also. Eggman has found a moon that can bend time, thus consequently going back in time and making the world into his own that way. Cheat. Sonic finds out and stops him as always. There are many secrets in this game, and the different backdrops as you travel back and forth in time are stunning.

See more information on Sonic CD - Mega CD

Sonic the Hedgehog Chaos - Master System/Game Gear
November 1993

Sonic Chaos was one of the last Sonic game on the Master System, before the surprise release of Sonic Blast, but there was more to come on the Game Gear. For the third game, Sega had totally given the graphics an overhaul from the last two games, and have included the Spin Dash, and Tails as a playable character, which makes the game even more exciting. In this game, Eggman has stolen the Chaos emeralds from South Island, and Sonic & Tails must stop him before the whole world is destroyed by the chaos that occurs without the Emeralds' power.

See more information on Sonic Chaos - MS/GG

Sonic the Hedgehog Spinball - Mega Drive
November 1993

No real sequel was released this year on the Mega Drive. No, Sonic Team had a much better treat in store for the Mega Drive owners. This Sonic game was a great little break from the platforming antics that the hedgehog is usually involved in. This was pinball - with a difference. Chaos Emeralds were the key to the exit, with a boss on each of the four levels, this game certainly wasn't easy.

See more information on Sonic Spinball - Mega Drive

Sonic Drift - Game Gear
November 1993

A change from the norm, this was a racing game starring Sonic and a small party of his buddies. You raced in cars along famous levels such as Emerald Hill Zone, and used weapons and dirty tactics in order to get ahead of the pack and win that trophy!

See more information on Sonic Drift - Game Gear
