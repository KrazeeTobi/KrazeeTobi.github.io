*Game Information*

Sega Sonic Arcade is an arcade game that no-one outside Japan has a clue about, not even me. This was released on the Model 2 Arcade Machine in the land of the rising sun, and never entered the west at all. The only machine seen working that wasn't in Japan was one spotted in London, although it was still just the Japanese version; no plans to bring it to the UK then :(

From all information and interpreting the available screenshots so far, Sega Sonic Arcade was its real name, despite many who believed the title was Sonic Arcade Game. It featured Sonic the Hedgehog, 'new' character Ray the Squirrel, and another new character Mighty the Armadillo, who found fame in the US and UK alongside Knuckles and the rest of the Chaotix team in the UK Sonic The Comic, and Sega 32X game Knuckles Chaotix. Players controlled their character with Trackballs (yeah, I dunno what they are either) built into the arcade machine as the team fought their way through the levels to beat Robotnik. Looking at the screenshots, it seems like you could get two of your buddies dishing out the pennies too, for three player simultaneous action. Unfortunately, absolutely nothing else is known about this mysterious game. Hopefully, when the emulated Sega Sonic Arcade ROM and Emu actually; a) appear or; b) work fast enough to be physically playable, Dreadknux can get some more in-game info of the Arcade game. That's assuming Sega are generous enough to put it on the upcoming Sonic 'Jam' Dreamcast compilation. It could happen, there's rumours about Sonic X-Treme appearing in there, so fingers crossed.
