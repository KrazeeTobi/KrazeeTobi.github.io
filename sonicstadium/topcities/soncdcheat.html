*Level Select* Mega CD

At the title screen press Up, Down, Down, Left, Right and B. You can choose your starting level. Once you finish a level you will go back to the title screen.

*Sound Test and Sound Test Codes* Mega CD

At the title screen press Down, Down, Down, Left, Right and A to access the Sound Test. Once you are there you can set these values for the following secrets:

*Time Attack Secrets* Mega CD

If you have already completed the game, you can access some secrets if you play the levels again and beat certain Time Attack totals:
Qualifying TimeEffect
37'27"57A new option will appear called "DA Garden". This lets you manipulate a picture of the Little Planet. The Control Pad moves, B rotates the camera, C Zooms, A chooses the music, and Start exits the mode.
30'21"05Press Left at the Time Attack screen to play the bonus rounds in Time Attack Mode.
25'46"12A new option will appear called "Visual Mode". This lets you see all the cartoons and a hidden pencil test too.

*Manipulate the Clouds* Mega CD

At the title screen hold down A and press Up, Down, Down, Down, Down and Up. Then you can use Controller 2 to manipulate the clouds. Press A and C to rotate them, B speeds them up, Up and Down tilt the horizon and Left and Right zooms the background in and out.
