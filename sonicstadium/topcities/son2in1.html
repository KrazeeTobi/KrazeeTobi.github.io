*Introduction*

This is a Game Gear game, that essentially has Sonic the Hedgehog 2, and Sonic Spinball in it. That's it. I can't really talk about this cartridge in any more detail, because you can check out the relevant Info pages to look at the two games. Get it if you don't have the two Game Gear games, but seriously this isn't really a necessity.

*In Depth Information*

This game has Sonic 2 combined in it. If you want to find out more about Sonic 2, click here to see info on Sonic 2 on the Master System/Game Gear.

This game has Sonic Spinball combined in it. There are quite obvious differences, and to be quite honest I don't really like the Game Gear version. I have played the first level, and it's pretty similar to the Mega Drive version, only the furthest I got was the top part of the first bit of the level. Controls are kinda awkward, and its basically... pants. Try the Mega Drive version, its much better.
