*Introduction*

Sonic & Knuckles Collection on the Sega PC is basically Sonic 3 & Knuckles again ... only on PC, of course. It may just be my copy though, but my S&K Collection seems to be preeety damn fast. Think of Sonic 3 & Knuckles at about six times its normal speed. And then the music quite oddly goes at 'normal' speed. One of the differences (if the speed thing is just my copy) is that the music is not the official Sonic Team music used on the Mega Drive versions. Instead we get midi files playing renditions of the same tunes.

*In Depth Information*

This game is exactly the same as Sonic 3 & Knuckles, apart from the minor differences explained above. Click here to see info on Sonic 3 & Knuckles.
