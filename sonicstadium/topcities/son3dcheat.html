*Level Select* Mega Drive

At the 'Sonic 3D flickie's Island' title screen press B, A, Right, A, C, Up, Down, and A. This will take you to the options screen (Start, Sound Test, Controls). On this screen go to Start. You'll be taken to the Level select.

*Easy Chaos Emeralds*

Once you have done the level select (above), if you collect 50 rings and meet either Tails or Knuckles you can skip Chaos Emerald stages by pressing Start and then A. You'll gain the Chaos emerald without even having to complete the stage.

*Level Skip* Mega Drive

Once you have activated the Level Select above, if you wish afterwards you can skip stage by pressing Start and then A.

*Level Skip* Sega Saturn

To enable the cheat mode, hold Up-Left + A + C at the Sonic 3D title screen. While holding these press Start, then choose 'start' as normal. To use the cheat mode, pause the game and press the following buttons for the corresponding effect:

A - skip to next Act
B - skip to next Stage
C - skip to the final boss
X - gain 1 life
Y - gain 1 sonic heads (10 sonic heads = 1 continue)
Z - gain 1 Chaos Emerald (collect all seven for the better ending)
