*Level Select*

At the Title Screen press Up, Up, Up, Down, Down, Down, Left, Right, Left, Right. Then press A and START together.

*Debug Mode*

First, activate the Level Select, as explained above. Go to the Sound Test and play sounds 01, 09, 09, 04, 01, 00, 01, 08 in that order. Highlight the level you want and press A and START together. However, in ebug mode you can only place a single ring and a ring monitor on each level.
