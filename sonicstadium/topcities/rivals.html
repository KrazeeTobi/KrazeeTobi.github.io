Sega's rivals!!!

In the past, Sega have had tough competition from an old respected rival called Nintendo. Consequently, Sonic and Mario were constantly fighting for Number One spot in people's lives (of course the hedgehog won!). But aside from Ninty, who have been Sega and Sonic's rivals, past and new? Well, here's the lowdown on the bad boys.

Nintendo
A foe of days past, Nintendo and Sega were constantly fighting for success during the early to mid 1990's (ah those were the days... ^_^), and I must say Ninty pulled off some impressive games such as Zelda and Starwing (Starfox in US). While Nintendo conquered in Japan, it was the Mega Drive that ruled supreme with the west side of the globe. Despite their game rivalry in the past, the two companies have nothing but the utmost respect for each other, and Sonic Team are to start developing all major titles in the future for GameCube, so Sonic fans know what the next console is now!

Mario
What came from Nintendo's bowels brought forth their mascot, Mario 'Mario' Mario. It was Mario who started off the whole platforming thing, but all other companies that followed the genre was just pointless rip-offs of the game. Until Sonic the Hedgehog came along that was. It was the entrance of Sonic that Mario had a feud on his fat little hands, and even now, since Sonic Team are developing for GameCube, there's even more mascot rivalry going on for best game character. But, with games released at totally different times, and a Mario game hardly appearing after Mario 64, it doesn't seem that Sonic moving platforms would make a difference.

Not really a contender against Sonic, or Mario for that matter, Crash Bandicoot was Sony's only hope to bring imaginitive mascot platforming to their consoles. Not that the games were imaginitive anyway - the levels were linear, but in its defence, I sort of like it - and now that Universal, the company responsible for Crash, has fallen out with Sony, Sony may be heading for a tumble. Although I haven't heard news of a new Crash game yet...
