1991
The year of the platform revolution!

<<1991 | 1992 | 1993 | 1994 | 1995 | 1996 | 1997 | 1998 | 1999 | 2000 | 2001>>

The year 1991, where it all started. Surely no-one expected the release of Sonic the Hedgehog, and when people found out about it, everybody across the country went to grab a shiny new Mega Drive and a copy of the platform hopping video game. Here's the games that came out this year:

Sonic the Hedgehog - Mega Drive
June 23rd, 1991

The Mega Drive game was released upon the unexpected public on this very day. This game had it all for its time - speed, anti-gravity stages, stupidly big loops to run around. This was the game that revolutionised platform games - forever.

See more information on Sonic 1 - Mega Drive

Sonic the Hedgehog - Master System/Game Gear
November, 1991

The Master System & Game Gear version of Sonic 1 came a little later on, when Sega had released the Master System in 1986, a few years before the Mega Drive, and the Game Gear to combat the Game Boy. Of course, some of the features could not of been handled on 8-bit machines, but it still had a great element of speed, puzzle and waterfalls in it. It was a great game in its own right. The Game Gear version differed slighty from the Master System, all of which is explained in the Back Catalogue.

See more information on Sonic 1 - MS/GG
