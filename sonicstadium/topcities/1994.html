1994
The year of the echidna!

<<1991 | 1992 | 1993 | 1994 | 1995 | 1996 | 1997 | 1998 | 1999 | 2000 | 2001>>

1994 was Sonic's peak time on the Mega Drive, and he released a few killer games which makes you glad you were livin' in the 90's. Baby. Ahem. This year was the great introduction to Knuckles, everyone's favourite echidna. He made his first appearance in Sonic 3, the big one for the Mega Drive, then went onto Sonic & Knuckles, then Game Gear. Here's the lowdown of the game:

Sonic the Hedgehog 3 - Mega Drive
February, 1994

Sonic makes his big comeback on the Mega Drive, and Sonic Team have bettered the graphics for this game. Knuckles first appears here, but is not playable, more a nuisance. This game was supposed to be bigger, hence Sonic & Knuckles was released as the second part to Sonic 3.

See more information on Sonic 3 - Mega Drive

Sonic & Knuckles - Mega Drive
October, 1994

Sonic & Knuckles and Sonic 3 were actually meant to be one whole game, but due to cartridge space S&K was released on a special lock-on cart, which works with Sonic 3 to give you that very big game that Sonic Team were supposed to give you in the first place. You can play as Knuckles now, and the game is backwards compatible with Sonic 2 and Sonic 1.

See more information on Sonic & Knuckles - Mega Drive

Sonic Triple Trouble - Game Gear
November, 1994

Sonic is back on the Game Gear, again with new and improved graphics and with the intro to Knuckles on the handheld, although you can't play as him. You can play as Sonic or Tails, and this is basically a sequel to Sonic Chaos, but much much better, which explains why this game is called Sonic & Tails 2 in Japan.
