Sonic Fan Fiction Club [SFFC]

Welcome to the Sonic Fan Fiction Club! If you fancy yourself as a bit of a budding script writer for the world's greatest hedgehog, and you want the world to see your work, this is the place to put them.

All kinds of Fan Fics are allowed here, you can submit your own written or typed stories, hand drawn cartoon/anime style comic Fics to Dreadknux, and you can do this by either attaching the file to an e-mail and E-mailing me or you can put it online yourself and tell me the link to your story using the form. You can also use the Form should your story be a short one, or an improvisation.

When Dreadknux recieves either a whole or part of a story, it will go online for the world to see it, text based or otherwise. As I mentioned, you don't have to send in the whole story at once, you can send it in by Chapters, it will give your readers a chance to look forward to the next Chapter. Most Fan Fics are happening in Dreadknux's Forum, so you too can take part in a big community participating Sonic story. All stories will be sent online, in this Zone.
