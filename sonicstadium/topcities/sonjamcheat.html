*New Time Attack*

At the title screen hold A and START while selecting Sonic World. Don't let go until you see Sonic appear. Go to the Challenges spot and you'll have a secret special challenge - collect 100 rings in 1 minute. Not a particularly tall order, is it?

*Clear Pause Screen*

Yeah, like no-one else has found this out before. If you haven't for some inane reason, while the game is paused hold down X, Y, and Z to get rid of the pause menu and get a clear screen.
