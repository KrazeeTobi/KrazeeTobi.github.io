Album Artwork

Yes indeedy, Sonic has hit the charts, probably not in the Top 40 or anything, but still he tried bless him. During 1995 (ah, memories...) Sonic was massive, so it was decided that he spawned several pop albums, with the likes of Backstreet Boys etc. Some albums starred Sonic in his own songs (who can forget "They Call Me Sonic"), and then there was the Video Game Albums, that basically had tracks from several Sega and Sonic games. Here is the gallery of Album covers, if you look closely on some, you can tell which tracks were good enough for Sonic's musical attire.
