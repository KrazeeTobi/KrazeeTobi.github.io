*Level Select* Game Gear

At the Sonic Chaos title screen, press Up, Up, Down, Down, Right, Left, Right, Left, Start. You'll hear the sound of a 1-Up monitor being destroyed. The Level Select screen will follow.

*Sound Test* Game Gear

At the Sonic Chaos title screen, press Down, Down, Up, Up, Left, Right, Left, Right, 1, 2, Start.

*Fireball Sonic* Game Gear

First, you must have activated the Sound Test as explained above. Then, while playing, press Down, Down-Right, Right, Right + 1. Sonic will shoot a fireball.
