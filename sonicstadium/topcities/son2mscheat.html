*Level Select* Game Gear

The Easy Way
Turn on your Game Gear. When the SEGA logo appears, hold Down-Left + 1 + 2 + Start. Don't let go of these buttons no matter what. Next you'll see the intro screen. Watch this, still holding the buttons until the title screen appears. Keep holding the buttons until you hear three ring sounds quickly, one after the other. This is your chance. Let go of everything now and press Start. You'll now be taken to the Level select. Failing that, you could try...

The Hard Way
After you turn on the Game Gear, hold Down-Left + 1 + 2. Hold these buttons throughout the intro sequence, where Tails gets captured. When the title screen appears, you'll notice that Tails blinks once, then he blinks twice in a row. Still holding the above buttons, wait until Tails blinks twice in a row. The instant he blinks the second time press Start. You'll see that the screen freezes for a second and you'll hear a ring sound. You'll then be taken to the Level Select.

*Unlimited Lives* Master System/Game Gear

In Act 1 of Sky High Zone, there are two 1-ups in this act. The first one can be achieved by passing the bridge at the start, jumping over the two sets of spikes, and finally the third set, forgetting the rings and the spring. To get the second 1-Up box, go back to the spring you forgot and jump on it. Next walk into the horizontal spring, which will destroy the bricks as you spin. Get to the very end and you'll meet one horizontal spring and a vertical one. Jump on the vertical spring, and you see another spring. Land on this one, and hold left until you land on a high platform with pine trees in the background. Collect the second life. Take it, then die. Repeat the above for max lives.
