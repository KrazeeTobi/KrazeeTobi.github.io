This page is to celebrate the special occasion that is Sonic's Birthday - Today, on June 23rd, 1991, the first Sonic game was released upon a crowd of unsuspecting video gamers. It was due to Sonic's coolness and the way that the Sonic games instantly made platform games fashionable, plus with the backing of the greatest games developer in the world, that Sonic the Hedgehog became more popularly known in the video game household. Very soon, cartoons were spawned, figures manufactured and comics, music and god knows what else were created, sold, and loved by many fans out there. And even now, after the horrific times that Sega has been through after the Mega Drive, Sonic has still been the world's most popular video game character. This page is dedicated to that special hedgehog, and Sega. you'll find a whole 10 year history of Sonic up to the present day, unforgettable Sonic moments, where you can send your moments in too, Sonic's rivals battling for platforming crown, and what the future may hold for Sonic, including info on rumour Sonic games that may be released in the near future. Take your pick at the top of the page.
