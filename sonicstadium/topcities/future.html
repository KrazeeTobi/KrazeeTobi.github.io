What will be the future for Sonic the Hedgehog?

The rate that Sega are continuing, Sonic is set to win back some old fans of yesteryear. With Sega going multi-platform, Sonic is going to be able to spin dash his way onto all the upcoming next-gen consoles and although hardcore Sonic fans won't like the sound of that, it will be Sonic that will win back everyone and gain their trust back into the best console developer that is Sega.

Here are the current rumours surrounding future Sonic games. These are either true but still in the pipeline or total rumours, so I am advising you all now not to get your hopes up - Sega have cancelled games before, y'know. ^_^

Sonic Adventure - Nintendo GameCube
Whether this will be the first or second outing of the blue one will have to be decided, but one of the Adventures is rumoured to be heading to Ninty's new fancy gaming box. Stay tuned.

Sonic 'Jam' - Sega Dreamcast
Obviusly the title above isn't what it may be called, but Sega have announced a Sonic Jam style game for the Dreamcast. Given the amount of size a GD-ROM is, there's more than enough room to hold most, if not all of the Sonic games currently in existence. Rumours have it that Sonic X-Treme may even make an appearance in there. Dreadknux will have the latest as soon as the word is out.
