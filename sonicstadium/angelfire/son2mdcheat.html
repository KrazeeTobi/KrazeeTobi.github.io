*Level Select*

Go to options menu on the title screen and then go to Sound Test. Play sounds 19, 65, 09, and then 17 in that order. Press Start and the Mega Drive will reset. Now on the title screen hold down A and go into a level select, with a special sound test mode. From here you can choose your starting level and listen to in game music aswell.

*Debug Mode*

First activate the level select. Next, on the level select screen go to the special sound test option and play tunes 1, 9, 9, 2, 1, 1, 2, and 4. Now highlight the level that you want to go to and then press Start and A at the same time. Press B to be a sprite, C to place the sprite, and A to change the sprite. Once a sprite press B again to turn back into Sonic.

*Super Sonic*

First activate the Level Select. Now on the level select screen go to the sound test mode and play tunes 04, 01, 02, and 06. Select any level, collect 50 rings and then jump. Sonic will transform into his alter-ego, but be warned. Whereas Super Sonic cannot be harmed by badniks or spikes etc, he can still drown and get crushed.

*Four Continues*

First activate the level select. Then on the normal sound test options play tunes 1, 1, 2, and 4. Select the character select (Sonic, Tails or Sonic & Tails) and press Start.
