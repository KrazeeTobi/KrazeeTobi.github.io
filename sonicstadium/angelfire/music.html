Welcome to the Music Zone. Find all of your favourite Sonic choons in this section right here. We have everything from Sonic Team music to Sonic midis that talented people have created.

Right, I've linked all of the Music you need into just one section, so you don't need to go hunting in the different sections for midis and wavs and whatever. DO bear in mind though that the choons under the 'Sonic Team Music' section on each music page are © Sega and Sonic Team, and all rights are respected and reserved. However, on another note, under the 'Sega Sonic Music' section on each music page are midis which Sonic fans have created by themselves. In the same circumstances, all music found under that section are © of their respected owners, and in turn do not belong to Sega, only the original wavs and music under the 'Sonic Team Music' section.

Got that cleared up? Good. Now, if you want to send in any of your fine midi work, don't hesitate to send them to me via the email address in the main menu bar. Include the FULL title of your midi, along with your name and email address (optional). All that behind us, let's get jammin'!
