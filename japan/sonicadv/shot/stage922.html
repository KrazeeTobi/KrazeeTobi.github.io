Sonic Adventure Stages!

Sonic Adventure
After a successful production presentation on August 22,
Sonic Adventure is being developed at a rapid pace
to be exhibited at the Tokyo Game Show!

For those of you who couldn't make it to the presentation,
here are the three stages we played at the event,
along with the characters that appear in the game!

SPEED HIGHWAY
A high-speed stage running through Station Square at night, lined with skyscrapers! Supersonic Sonic runs from building to
building in all directions!

ICECAP
Snowboard through the silvery world!
Tails' game objective is to race against Sonic.
A heated race against Sonic in a world of snow and ice!

RED MOUNTAIN
Red Mountain, a scorching mountainous area spewing magma.
Knuckles used the sounds and colors of the radar to find the three
Master Emerald shards to clear the stage!
