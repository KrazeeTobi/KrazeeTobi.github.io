Sonic Team's Best Album Released!
SONIC TEAM "Power Play"
- BEST SONGS FROM SONIC TEAM -
Release date: November 6, 1998
Price: 2,310 yen
Product number: MJCA-00033
Distributed by: Marvelous Entertainment Inc.
Distributed by: Pony Canyon Inc.

The long-awaited best album of vocal songs from Sonic Team's works will finally be released on November 6th!
It includes vocal songs by Mitsuyoshi and Sasaki, as well as numerous English vocal songs.
It's a must-listen for its speedy, danceable sound on one disc.

Before we look to the future with Sonic Adventure, let's go back to our roots by listening to this first!
Seriously, we must sell out!

SEGA EUROPE's sound creator Richard wrote all the music for Sonic R, and this is the song that tops the list! Everybody's Super Sonic Racing !!

Anyway, Sonic R has a lot of melodious songs, and this is one of them! The melodies and rhythms will have you humming along before you know it!

Sonic R's ending song! A much calmer shuffle beat than the previous two songs! It's too cool! Number One, indeed!

[BURNING RANGERS]
06. Burning Hearts -ANGEL of Flame-
This is the theme song of the same title, sung passionately and enthusiastically by Mitsuyoshi of Sega. It's included in Sega Kara! I hope you all remember it and sing it.

07. We are Burning Rangers
An insert song that was used in the demo screen and shooting aspect of the game. Marlon Sanders, who sang on the acappella version of Dreams Dreams is on vocals. Burning Ranger is Omar Hakim's drumming in all the vocal songs. You can hear his hot playing especially in this song.

08. I just smile
The ending theme of the same song. The lyrics and vocals were written by Tomoko Sasaki of NiGHTS fame. The song has a delicate feel that fits the atmosphere of the character Ilia. It is a heart-wrenching mid-ballad.

[SONIC CD]
09. Sonic: You Can Do Anything
The theme song for the opening movie of the MEGA CD version of Sonic CD, released in 1993. It was sung by Keiko Utoku. It is a pop number with a sense of speed that matches the movie of Sonic running around.

10. Cosmic Eternity: Believe in Yourself
The ending theme of the same song. This is also sung by Utoku. Both the opening and ending theme are lyrics and RAP by forumer SHOGUN guitarist Casey Rankin. It is written with a positive and powerful theme.
