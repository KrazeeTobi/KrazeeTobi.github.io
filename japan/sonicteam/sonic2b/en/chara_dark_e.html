Shadow the Hedgehog

A black hedgehog who resembles Sonic. He is the ultimate life form created by Professor Gerald. He has the ability to use his special power, "Chaos Control" and is able to harness the power of the Chaos Emerald to warp time and space. He tells Dr. Eggman about the Eclipse Cannon and persuades him to use it to take over the world, but what is Shadow's real motive? The ultimate life form never loses.

Dr. Eggman
w/ Egg Walker

He calls himself the genius evil scientist. With an IQ of 300, he is a renowned authority on robotics. He hates Sonic as a rival for life. His plans for world domination begin with collecting all of the Chaos Emeralds with the help of Shadow and Rouge. Only the victory proves his intelligence.

Rouge the Bat

She is a treasure hunter who specializes in jewels. She offers to help Eggman find the pieces of the Master Emerald, but no one knows her real identity. She is very proud of her work and is relentless in getting what she wants.
