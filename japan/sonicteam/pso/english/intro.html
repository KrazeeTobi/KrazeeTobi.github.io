 "In the year 2000, Sega's legendary RPG Phantasy Star"
was finally revived by Sonic Team!

When we first started designing this online game, 
we tried to come up with the best way for people to communicate and interact with each other. 
We decided to create an online game
based on the world of Sega's legendary RPG series Phantasy Star.

That was the moment that the concept of Sega's latest RPG, Phantasy Star Online, was born.

Sega has been trying to create the most exciting game world for players 
since the 8bit console Sega Mark 3/Sega Master System was introduced. 
Events, such as a dragon bursting out of the wall will thrill players 
when they travel in the Phantasy Star Online world. 
The amazing adventure in a realistic 3D world,
which Sega has not been able to achieve in the past Phantasy Star series, 
will be available on Dreamcast, SegaÅfs third generation console.
