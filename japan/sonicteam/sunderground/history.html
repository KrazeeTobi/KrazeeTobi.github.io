History Behind Sonic Underground

  It began just after Sonia, Manic, and Sonic were born.    Mobotropolis was a peaceful city back then, but the evil Doctor Robotnik soon used his technology to turn their world into a place of terror known as Robotropolis!

He left the wealthy aristocrats to pay for his smelly factories while their people were robotcized and became mindless slaves!

Their mother, Queen Aleena, took them into hiding after a price was placed on their heads!

Then one day, the Oracle of Delphius appeared to her and foretold a prophecy. He told her that she'd have to give them up to save their lives. It was a three-way split.

Sonia was raised by Lady Windimere, a kindly aristocrat...

...while Manic was raised by a good-natured thief named Farrell...

...and Sonic grew up with his Uncle Chuck, a top-notch inventor.

Seperated, they'd each become pretty good with certain musical intruments, but it wasn't until they jammed together as a band that the Oracle of Delphius revealed their true history.

After that, the trouble really started.

Not only did Robotnik manage to roboticize Sonic's Uncle Chuck, but he did the same to Farrell, and poor Lady Windimere!

Ever since that day, they formed a resistance group and went underground, 'till the day they can reunite with their mom to become the Council of Four, and overthrow Robotnik once and for all!
