Screen shots / Boss Battle
Powerful boss characters appear in the first half of the game.
Big Foot / SONIC THE HEDGEHOG
F-6t BIG FOOT

A anti-terrorist military fighter which has mechanical legs.

It's equipped with missile pods and hover jets to take off vertically and a rotary machine gun.

It's been called in by the government military to capture Sonic.

It's part of the Scorpion troop.

It fires homing missiles from both of its
missile pods.		 It's designed for striding as it chases Sonic. Stay on your toes to defeat this boss.
Hot Shot / SHADOW THE HEDGEHOG
B-3x HOT SHOT

A security defense military fighter with bi-pedal locomotion.

Hot Shot is more powerful than Big Foot and has stronger armaments. Not only does it have homing missiles, hover jets and a machine gun, but it's also equipped with a lock-on energy beam cannon.

The military is ordered to seek and destroy Shadow who has broken into the security area.

Hot Shot belongs to the Spider troop.

Chase down Shadow from the sky with the sweeping fire. Watch out, Shadow! Lock-on to the target! Fire the energy beam at full power!
Dr. Eggman / MILES "TAILS" PROWER
Dr.EGGMAN w/ EGGMOBILE

Tails versus Eggman's quick battle!

Eggman attacks Tails using the Egg Mobile's machine gun.

Tails always depends on Sonic, but this time he confronts Eggman alone.

If he gets behind you, run to your right and left. Wait for the right time to strike. Now's my chance! I've got luck on my side! Now you're gonna get it!
