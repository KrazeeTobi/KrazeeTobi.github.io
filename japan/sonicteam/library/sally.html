The Sally Moon Archives

   I have a special archive just for those special Sally Moon fan-fic writers, present company included (me).

  1. The Night of Starlight by Maurice Takeshi- Pretty much a tale of origin of how the Starlight Knight came to be. Prolougue to "The Beginning of Sally Moon" by Sally Acorn.
  2. The Beginning of Sally Moon by Sally Acorn-Originally going to be called "Elders", the storyline was changed. When Sally is invited by Bunnie to go to a bazaar at Sunrise Resort, she decides to see what this is all about. Upon reaching the village, Sally is unexpectfully greeted by an old friend. After clearing up a misunderstanding, the gang attends the city banquet for that night. But things take a turn for the worse when Sunrise Resort is attacked by a powerful monster. Wait till the new heroine shows up.

ATTENTION ALL FAN FIC WRITERS: Submit a Sally Moon fan fic story and have it placed here in the Sally Moon Achives! Just make sure the story follows the general timeline of the Sally Moon stories by Sally Acorn and Maurice Takeshi. And no killing off characters made by Sally or Archie Comics. Thank you!
