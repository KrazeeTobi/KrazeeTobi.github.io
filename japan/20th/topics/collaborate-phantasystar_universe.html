Sonic is coming to Phantasy Star Universe!

"Phantasy Star Universe: The Ambition of Illuminus", one of Sega's most popular online RPGs, will hold an in-game event to celebrate Sonic's birthday, "Sonic's Birthday 2011", on June 23.

This year, the Guardians Colony of Phantasy Star Universe will once again be home to the Sonic Lobby, which will be decorated to celebrate his birthday.

Sonic and Tails will also be visiting the Graal Solar System on their birthday, June 23rd, from 8pm to 10pm, and will be traveling between lobbies and universes throughout Graal to take birthday photos with the Guardians!

"Phantasy Star Universe: The Ambition of Illuminus" is a science fiction fantasy RPG for Windows PC that is currently available for free. You can participate in the event with free client software and free basic play, so why not join us?
