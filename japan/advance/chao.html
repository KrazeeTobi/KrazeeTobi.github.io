Sonic Advance



The world's first linkage with Sonic Adventure 2 Battle!

The Game Boy Advance version of Sonic Advance and the Nintendo GameCube version of Sonic Adventure 2 Battle are linkable!

You can move your Chao between the Game Boy Advance and GameCube, allowing you to raise your own Chao anytime, anywhere.

Tiny Chao Garden in Sonic Advance
You can buy nuts with rings you got in the main game,
and you can also get more rings in the mini-games Chao Memory and Rock, Paper, Scissors.
There are also Chao eggs that can only be found in the Tiny Chao Garden.
You can transfer Chao and nuts to the Chao Garden.
If you save your Chao to a cartridge, you can carry them around with you and train them.
*The image is from the Game Boy Advance version.

Chao Garden in Sonic Adventure 2 Battle


You can raise Chao by feeding them, giving them small animals or Chaos Drives obtained in the main game. You can love them, and you can even have them compete in races!

When you insert Chao into the machine, you can transfer it to the Tiny Chao Garden on the Game boy Advance.

You can transfer the Chao you grew in Tiny Chao Garden and the nuts you bought to the Chao Garden.
