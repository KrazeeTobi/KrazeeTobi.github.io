Sonic the Hedgehog

The world's fastest supersonic hedgehog.

He loves to live a free and easy life, and has a bit of an attitude. He has a kind heart and won't leave anyone in need alone. His controls are well-balanced, and we recommend it for beginners and pros alike.
Miles "Tails" Prower

Sonic's sidekick, a little fox.

He has a gentle personality and is a big fan of mechanics. Tails' greatest trait is his ability to fly by spinning his two tails, which is also the origin of his nickname. By flying through the air, you can easily explore the zones. Tails is sure to be able to find the Special Spring in even the most obscure of places.

Knuckles the Echidna

Sonic's best friend and rival, an echidna.

He's a powerful echidna that can shatter boulders with a single blow. He has a single-minded and serious personality. Knuckles' greatest abilities is that he can glide and climb walls. Thanks to these abilities, he has a wider range of movement than Sonic. This makes him a good character for exploring maps.

Amy Rose

Sonic's self-proclaimed girlfriend, who's always full of energy.

With her beloved Piko Piko Hammer as her weapon, she helps Sonic. Unlike the other characters, Amy cannot spin, which can be a little challenging if you've played with the other characters. Among the four characters, Amy is the one for pro players.

Dr. Eggman

A scientific genius who sees Sonic as his rival.

He's attempted many evil schemes, most of which thwarted by Sonic. This time, his goal is to build an empire of his own. In order to do so, he has turned innocent animals into diabolical badniks using his scientific skills. He's not very good at physical combat, so he rides on his own machines.
