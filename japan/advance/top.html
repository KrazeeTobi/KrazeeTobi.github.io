Sonic's coming to the Game Boy Advance!
In celebration of Sonic's 10th anniversary, he's arrived on the Game Boy Advance!

 This is the latest side-scrolling action game in the series, reminiscent of Sonic's origins, befitting the 10th anniversary of the franchise. The overwhelming sense of speed, the variety of stages and gimmicks, and the unique characters are the very essence of the Sonic franchise. *Sonic the Hedgehog turned 10 years old on June 23, 2001

Since his first game in June 1991 for the Mega Drive (Genesis), the Sonic franchise has been so popular, that there have been over 25 million sales worldwide.

His trusty friends Tails, Knuckles, and Amy are also here!

In this side-scrolling action game, you must defeat enemies and collect rings in order to reach the Chaos Emerald. Tails, Knuckles, and Amy also make an appearance as reliable companions. Sonic's high-speed dashing and rolling attacks, Tails' flying by spinning his tails, Knuckles' powerful gliding, and Amy's jumping and attack with her bouncy hammer are all unique actions, join forces to crush Dr. Eggman's ambitions!





These actions, which have been difficult to reproduce in portable games until now, have been completely recreated in this title! Four players can battle each other on one cartridge, and each of the four characters will have a blast!
