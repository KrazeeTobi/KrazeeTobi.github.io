SONIC 2 MODE (CODE)
Sonic and Tails work together to advance the adventure.Sonic 2 mode is its name.
In this mode, Tails will follow behind Sonic, who's controlled by the player.
Steps

1: Go to Sonic on the character select screen and press the D-Pad up once.
2: Next, go to Tails and press the D-Pad down once.
3: Next, go to Knuckles and press the L button once.
4: Next, go to Amy and press the R button once.
5: Finally, move the cursor over "Sonic" again and enter the [A] button.

If you hear the sound of a ring being taken when you decide on the player character, the code was successful.

--Notes--
In Sonic 2 mode, you can go to the EXTRA ZONE if you meet the requirements, but Tails will not follow you to the EXTRA ZONE or the Special Stage. This code isn't valid in Battle or Time Attack mode.
