SONIC2 MODE (COMMAND)
Sonic and Tails work together to advance the adventure."Sonic 2 mode"it is.
In this mode, Tails will follow behind Sonic, who is controlled by the player.
Steps

1: Move the cursor to "Sonic" on the character select screen and press the cross button [UP] once.
2: Next, place the cursor on "Tails" and press the cross button [DOWN] once.
3: Next, move the cursor to "Knuckles" and press the [L] button once.
4: Next, place the cursor on "Amy" and press the [R] button once.
5: Finally, move the cursor over "Sonic" again and enter the [A] button.

If you hear the sound of a ring being taken when you decide on the player character, the command input was successful.

--Points to note--
In Sonic 2 mode, you can go to the EXTRA ZONE if you meet the requirements, but Tails will not follow you to the EXTRA ZONE or the Special Stage. Also, this command is not valid in Battle or Time Attack mode.
