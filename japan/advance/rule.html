CHARACTER SELECT
　 　 　
Each character has different abilities, so the game can be enjoyed in different ways.

ACT SELECT
Acts will start from "ZONE 1 ACT 1" for each player character you choose. For each player character, the clear data of the ZONE is saved separately, so you can only select the Act that you are challenging.

CLEAR METHOD
The requirements for clearing the Act differ between Act 1 and Act 2. In Act 1, you must pass through the clear panel, and in Act 2, you must defeat the boss and rescue the animals from the capsule to complete the Act.

BASIC SYSTEM
▼　Rings
[Always have at least one with you.]
If you have one of the rings available in the Act, you will not die even if you take damage from gimmicks or enemy attacks. However, all the rings you had will be scattered all over the field, so hurry up and pick them up.
▼　Point Markers
[Restart point after death.]
Point markers are placed at various locations in the Act. If you touch these point markers, you can restart from that point marker point even if you die in the Act.
▼　1up
[How to increase the number of lives]
If you collect 100 rings or get a 1UP item, the number of lives will increase by one. In taddition, for every 50,000 points scored, the number of lives will increase by one.
▼　Item Box
[Break them to get the items.]
Even if you find an item, you cannot get it unless you destroy the item box. Use special attacks to destroy it and get the item inside.
▼　Special Stage

[Let's collect the rings scattered around.]
Special Stages can be accessed by using special springs in the field. If you collect the specified number of rings in a special stage, you can get the Chaos Emerald.
▼　Death
[There are many dangers in the Act.]
If you take damage while not holding any rings, you will die and lose one life. In addition to damage, you may also die. (such as falling from a floorless area or not being able to replenish oxygen in the water)
▼　Continuation
[Let's try again to clear it.]
The number of lives decreases as you die, and when it reaches zero, the game is over. However, if you have enough continues left, you can try again.
