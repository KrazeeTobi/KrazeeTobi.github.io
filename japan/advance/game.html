GAME START ( 1P PLAY )
This is a one-player-only game mode.
In the main game of this game, you control the player character to clear each zone.
Many of the zones are divided into Act 1 and Act 2, with Act 2 being a confrontation with a boss mech piloted by Dr. Eggman.

VS (2 to 4P PLAY)

If you connect the Game Boy Advance communication cable, up to four players can enjoy the game at the same time.
All of us have cartidges."Multi-Cartridge Battle"と、
Play with a single cartridge."One Cartridge Match"You can enjoy.
▼　Multi-Cartridge Battle
You will need enough cartridges for the number of players, and you can play cooperatively in two teams.
▼　One cartridge match
If you have one cartridge, you can play against each other. The game you can play is "Collecting Rings".

TIME ATTACK (1P PLAY)
In this mode, the player selects a character and Act, and competes for the fastest time to clear the game.
Act 1 is to pass the clear panel, and Act 2 is to fight for time to open the capsule.

OPTIONS ( EXTRA )
▼　Personal data
You can register your name and check the results of your matches.
You can enter up to six alphanumeric characters for the name.
▼　Degree of difficulty
You can change the difficulty of the game.
There are two types: "Normal" and "Easy".
▼　Passing the deadline
You can change the down due to time loss in the game to "Yes" or "No".
▼　Listen to the sound
You can listen to the BGM in the game. There are 42 tracks in total.
▼　Choose one's language
You can choose between "Japanese" and "English".
▼　Button operation
You can change the operation of the A and B buttons. Normal is the initial state, while reverse will switch the operation.
▼　Erase all records
You can delete all data except for "Chao's Petit Garden" and initialize the saved data.

SPECIAL STAGE ( 1P PLAY )
▼　Chao's Petit Garden

You can feed your Chao with nuts and other things to make them grow. Also, the GameCube version "Sonic Adventure 2 Battle" , allows you to transfer the Chao you have grown.
