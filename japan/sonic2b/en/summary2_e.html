SONIC ADVENTURE 2 BATTLE
 Product information / About Sonic Adventure 2 Battle
The new theme, Battle, heats up the 2-Player mode!
The new theme for the Nintendo GameCube version is "Battle."
The ultimate battle between the Hero side and the Dark side is even more fun! Everything is new and there is more to enjoy in the new version.
Characters for the battle are added, and the battle mode is even more exciting!
Six popular characters from the previous Sonic series, including Amy, Metal Sonic, and Chaos 0 have been added as playable characters for the 2-player battle as well as the familiar six Hero and Dark characters.

A total of 12 characters are available for the 2-player battle and they have their own unique powers. Enjoy a different battle each time!

Six different battles now available!
Besides Sonic's high-speed action races, six new battles have been added.
* You need to use deductive reasoning and observation in Knuckles' treasure hunting.
* You can enjoy the exciting shooting in Tails' battles.
* Try drift driving in Kart racing.
* Your favorite Chao will compete against your friend's Chao in the Chao race.
* The new martial art battle, Chao Karate, is now available.
Various 2-player battle lineups are sure to satisfy all players.
New stages and systems have been added. Let's enjoy the battle with everyone!
New battle stages have been added for 2-player battle mode for your enjoyment. Each stage contains battles levels 1 to 4, and players can select the level that they want depending on their skills. Level 1 battle is easy enough for small children. You need to use special techniques to win level 3 battle. Level 4 battle is a secret stage, which is difficult to clear. The auto handicap system makes each battle more exciting for players whose technique levels are different.
