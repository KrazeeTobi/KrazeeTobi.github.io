The next 2P battle mode is shooting battle, where the player who destroys the other first wins. The rules are very simple. The player can use the ability of each machine to their advantage. Also use traps or obstacles in the stage to win. There is a short-distance race and a short-distance obstacle course in this game mode. In the short-distance race, the player who gets to the goal first wins. In the short-distance obstacle course, the players must avoid or destroy obstacles in their way to get to the goal.
The basic controls are the keys to winning. The obstacle sprint course. It's important to know the advantages and disadvantages of each character's machine to win.
Get a clear picture of the map and hunt down your opponent. Then, defeat him using your special attack! Chao also participate in this battle!
