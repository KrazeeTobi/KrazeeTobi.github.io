Dr. Eggman breaks into the military base looking for the top-secret weapon, Shadow. However, what he finds is a black hedgehog. The black hedgehog, Shadow calls himself the ultimate life form.
Shadow invites Dr. Eggman to the space colony, Ark, and persuades him to use the Eclipse Cannon to take over the world. Then, a mysterious batgirl, Rouge, appears in front of them and offers her help.
World conquest now begins with these three characters.
