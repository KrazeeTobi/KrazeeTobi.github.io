The third battle mode is treasure hunting. The goal is to find the pieces of the Master Emerald that are hidden somewhere in the stage. The player who finds the pieces first wins. Find the pieces using hints and radar. The locations of the pieces are hidden and change every time you play. You can enjoy playing this mode again and again. You can also turn the tables using special attacks, making the 2P battle more exciting than ever.
There are various stages for treasure hunting, such as ruins, oasis, safe rooms, and outer space. In order to win, it's important to know where your opponent is.
It's important to use any special actions as much as possible, such as climbing on the wall or gliding.It all depends on which stage you are on. Understand each character's advantage and use them effectively. It's the fastest way to win.
