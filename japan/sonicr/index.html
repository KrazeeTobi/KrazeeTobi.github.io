Game Features

An action-packed race featuring Sonic and others!
In this game, Sonic and other Sonic characters appear.
Sonic's Spin Dash, Tails' Flight, and other Sonic characters have special abilities that we're all familiar with. The player must use those abilities from each character to finish the race.
Furthermore, there are hidden characters that can be played as, adding depth to the strategy of each character.
Course layout with many branching routes, the exhilaration os running through the field freely
The course is not just a route along the road. There are various branching routes that await the player along the way. In addition to racing along the road, players can also enjoy the exhilaration of running around freely in the field.
In addition, the field has tricks and routes that take advantage of the characteristics of each character, so players can have fun attack each course and finding all the branching routes.
A variety of fricks and plenty of fun on the course
There are various tricks on the course, such as familiar loops and routes that can only be taken by collecting rings. This is the first action-racing game that is like this.
In addition, Chaos Emeralds and hidden characters are hidden in the course, and players will have fun discovering and collecting them.
