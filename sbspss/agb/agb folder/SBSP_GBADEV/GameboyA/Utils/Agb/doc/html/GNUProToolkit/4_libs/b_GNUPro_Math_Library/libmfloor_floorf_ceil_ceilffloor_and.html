floor, floorf, ceil, ceilf
[floor and ceiling]

SYNOPSIS

    #include <math.h>
    double floor(double x);
    float floorf(float x);
    double ceil(double x);
    float ceilf(float x);
DESCRIPTION
    floor and floorf find x the nearest integer less than or equal to x.

    ceil and ceilf find x, the nearest integer greater than or equal to x.

RETURNS
    floor and ceil return the integer result as a double. floorf and ceilf return the integer result as a float.
COMPLIANCE
    floor and ceil are ANSI. floorf and ceilf are extensions.
