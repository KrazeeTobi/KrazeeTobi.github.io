Selecting cross references
We have already discussed the ‘Next’, ‘Prev’, and ‘Up’ pointers which appear at the top of a node. In addition to these pointers, a node may contain other pointers which refer you to a different node, perhaps in another info file. Such pointers are called cross references, or xrefs for short. See also Parts of an Xref and Selecting Xrefs.
