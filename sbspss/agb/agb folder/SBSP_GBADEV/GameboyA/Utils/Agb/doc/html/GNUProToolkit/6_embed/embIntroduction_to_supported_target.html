Overview of supported targets for cross-development
The following documentation describes programming practices and options for several of the embedded targets supported by the GNUPro Toolkit. Since, by their very nature, the tools are evolving to meet the needs of customers, find the new targets in the matrix (see Embedded cross-configuration support and Native configuration support in Introduction in Getting Started with GNUPro Toolkit, for the host/target combinations that Cygnus supports).
