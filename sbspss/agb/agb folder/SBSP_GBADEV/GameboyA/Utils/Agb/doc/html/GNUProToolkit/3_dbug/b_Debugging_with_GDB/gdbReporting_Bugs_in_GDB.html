Reporting bugs in GDB
Your bug reports play an essential role in making GDB reliable.

Reporting a bug may help you by bringing a solution to your problem, or it may not. But in any case the principal function of a bug report is to help the entire community by making the next version of GDB work better. Bug reports are your contribution to the maintenance of GDB.

In order for a bug report to serve its purpose, you must include the information that enables us to fix the bug. See Have you found a bug? and How to report bugs for more details.

For those customers using the GNUPro debugger, Cygnus InsightTM, or the GNUPro debugging tools, see http://support.cygnus.com.
