expm1, expm1f
[exponential minus 1]

SYNOPSIS


    #include <math.h>
    double expm1(double x);
    float expm1f(float x);

DESCRIPTION


    expm1 and expm1f calculate the exponential of x and subtract 1, that is, ex – 1 (where e is the base of the natural system of logarithms, approximately 2.71828).

    The result is accurate even for small values of x, where using exp(x)-1 would lose many significant digits.

RETURNS


    ex – 1.

COMPLIANCE


    Neither expm1 nor expm1f is required by ANSI C or by the System V Interface Definition (Issue 2).
