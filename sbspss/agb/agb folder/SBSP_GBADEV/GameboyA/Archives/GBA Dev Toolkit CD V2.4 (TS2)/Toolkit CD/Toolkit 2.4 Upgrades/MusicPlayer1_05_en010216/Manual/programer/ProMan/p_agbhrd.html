AGB System Sound Circuits

The AGB System comes equipped with a "Direct Sound" circuit that outputs sampled sounds in stereo and a "Compatible Sound" circuit that can output sound that is the equivalent of the conventional Game Boy(DMG/CGB). In this system, it is possible to use both simultaneously to play songs. An image of the AGB System Sound Circuits is shown below.
