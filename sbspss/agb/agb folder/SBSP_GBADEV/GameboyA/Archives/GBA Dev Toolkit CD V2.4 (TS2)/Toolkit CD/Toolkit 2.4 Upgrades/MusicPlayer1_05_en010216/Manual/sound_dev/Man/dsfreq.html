Direct Sound Playback Frequencies

The direct sound playback frequencies may be chosen from the following levels.  
   5734 Hz,  7884 Hz, 10512 Hz, 13379 Hz, 15768 Hz, 18157 Hz,
  21024 Hz, 26758 Hz, 31536 Hz, 36314 Hz, 40137 Hz, 42048 Hz

Note: Increasing the playback frequencies will increase the processing time.
