How to Operate the Previewer

This section describes how to control the Previewer from the AGB.

Switching Modes

SELECT button . . . Switch between control of objects and control of BG

Controlling Objects

A button . . . Switch animations
START button . . . Initialize scaling, rotation and motion

When the B button is not pressed:
Control Pad . . . Move object
LR buttons . . . Switch objects

While the B button is being pressed:
Control Pad . . . Scale object
LR buttons . . . Rotate object

Controlling BG

When the B button is not pressed:
Control Pad . . . Move BG
LR buttons . . . Switch BG

While the B button is being pressed (Only for scaling-rotating screens)
Control Pad . . . Scale BG
LR buttons . . . Rotate BG

Notes

The SELECT button has no effect when previewing only objects or only BG.
