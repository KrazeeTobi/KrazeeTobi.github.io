Special reports happen in case of some kind of event or around the world report. we'll have a lot of exclusive coverage on events such as the San Diego Comic Con. This time around, Knux Lee of Sonic HQ brings us a report about how Sonic is faring in parts of Southern Asia (Malaysia and Singapore). People there are not into Sonic as the people in America and Europe, keep that in mind. You can contact the writer via Mobius Forum of Sonic HQ.

Special Report: 11-30-98 by Knux Lee
Hi, this is Knux Lee here with my report on Sonic fandom in Malaysia and well, sorta includes Singapore in certain aspects.

Malaysia Sonic Fandom on GAMES

I can say, many love the Sonic games. However, not many own Megadrives / Genesises or Saturns. They instead play the Nintendo Sonic hacks. Some of these hacks include ones starring Mario. Get the picture? Mario running as fast as Sonic? I believe the 'Hidden Sonic Secrets' page has a rom of this but on Megadrive I believe. Also, the Nintendo version of Sonic has lousy graphics, as Nintendo and it's clones only are 8-bit machines. And these people that like the games, sadly, aren't real fans and don't give a care to who Sonic really is. Only a few do. Also, the console that rules the market here is probally the Playstation. I have met very few that own Saturns and only about THREE who own Genesises or Megadrive 2s. Also, there are silly games like 'Sonic and Knuckles 5' which actually is just Sonic 1 where (I believe) you can play Knuckles. And also, very few know about the Dream- cast except the hardcore gamers who have the money to buy magazines, us, the internet sonic fans, and the other gamers who have internet.

Malaysia Sonic Fandom on CARTOONS

Our satellite tv, ASTRO links to the (Malaysian) Disney Channel which shows both the Sonic cartoons, AoStH and Satam. Because of this, many like the Sonic cartoons but as the series is not completed, know little about Sonic and therefore aren't real fans. Sonic however, is 3 times a day and therefore is watched by those who watch cartoon after cartoon. (Like me, when I go to my grandparent's house a little south. I don't have ASTRO!)

Malaysian Sonic Fandom on COMICS

Sonic sells pretty well here, as when I visited our capital, Kuala Lumpur last year, not one shop of the tens I visited had a copy of Sonic. All said "Sorry, sold out." Here up north in Kedah, we have very few comic stores. Therefore, I get my comics from Popular Bookstore, a large bookstore with branches everywhere. (even singapore and etc.) Comics are quite hard to find as not many bookstores import comics. Comic bookstores also import more of the DC, Image and so on comics. And also, those who read the comics tend to clash with those who only watched the cartoons. Note : All this info is on Archie, not Fleetway. I haven't seen ONE Fleetway comic here..)

Malaysian Sonic Fandom on MISC

Well, I don't know what to put under MISC but all I can say is, the main factor that draws people to Sonic fandom is the games. Then slowly when they see the cartoons, it's even kewler! Then they venture into the Internet Sonicverse and meet us, after finding so many friends with similiar interests, they slowly develop more like for Sonic and then WHAM! They are fans. Those who DON'T venture into the Internet somehow lose interest after seeing other stuff. I lost my only fellow Sonic fan due to whaddya know, Hanson. (&%$*^!)

Summerize...

Sonic Fandom is small in Malaysia and therefore must be watered and fertilized with the Dreamcast and Sonic Adventure! Then, it will rule the world!
