Sonic News Links



Although we're the best (notice that I didn't say the fastest, but soon we will be!) source on the whole Internet for all kinds of Sonic related news (yeah, ONLY Sonic related news), there are quiet a few others out there. Here's some that I think are pretty good Sonic news pages. BTW, if you have a page that belongs down here, please email me the link, and I'll put it up.

Other Sonic News Pages!

Knuxcom News - Not very up to date, but a good archive that extend back to September of 1996.

Sonic the Hedgehog News - In depth coverage of all the important Sonic news, not updated very often, but a must visit if you're clueless about a few Sonic events.

Sonic-Related News - Not a bad place. But not very up to date. Check it out if you have time.

Mara-Re's Sonicverse News Room - Although a very simple design, it's one of my favorite Sonic news places...it's the only site that sometimes report news faster (I mean a lot faster) than us.

tSf News Board - An excellent place if you want a combination of Sega nd Sonic news on the run.

NEXCOM News - Well, just another news page. Not very up to date, but if you have time, take a look.

Dark Legion News - Pepper J. Zeal's page, not updated often, but enough to tell you what's going on.

Ken Penders' Page - Well, it's the writer's page, there gotta be something new...

Tiara's News Page - If you're looking for Sonic gaming news, go here. Just beware of the background color.
