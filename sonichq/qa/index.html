Getting Games

Where on the internet can I download sonic the hedgehog games?

SonicHQ does not carry Sonic games, however check the Emulation Zone home page (the link is on the main page) they might have some. - Ron Prower

Entire Comics?

Is there any website with scans of ALL the pages in a Sonic Comic?

Currently there is no site that has every single page of a comic that I know of. However try looking around "second-rate" sites as they sometimes have things the big sites don't have. - Ron Prower

Super Sonic for GG?

Is there a Super Sonic for Sonic 2 for Game Gear?

No even though there are the chaos emeralds, there is no Super Sonic for the Game Gear version of Sonic 2. - Ron Prower

12-05-01

Chao Life

How long do Chao stay alive becuase I was wondering how long it lives before it reverts back into an egg? My chao recently reverted into an egg before I got all my emblems!

It all depends on how you raise them, if you give them too many animals or chao drives at once they will quickly revert back. Also if you neglect them or mis treat them they will go back. If you care for the Chao healthfully it will last a long time. - Ron Prower

Finding Music

I played Sonic The hedehog (Sega) with my brother and enjoyed it at that time. The music of the first stage (1) keeps coming in my mind, and I'm trying to find it. The "green hills" is not the same music as I heard. Where am I wrong? Can you tell me where I can get that music from?

The first level of Sonic 1 is known as "Green Hill Zone" you can find multiple MIDI remixes at www.vgmusic.com, also I blieve our music section has it on the server. Sometimes "Green Hill Zone" may be refered to as "Emerald Hill Zone" though. - Ron Prower

Multiple Names

What other name is Dr. Robotnik known by?

Dr. Robotnik is also known as Eggman and as Julian Kintobar. - Ron Prower
