Well, if you're interested in the wedding angle, that has yet to be resolved. In fact, that will be addressed again in a few weeks when Sally actually does undergo that rite which King Acorn asked her to go through.

As a recap, let's quickly review what's happened until now:

> Sonic & Tails found and defeated Naugus thanks to the help of an overlander named Nate Morgan. Nate is an Overlander who was the real creator of the power ring and a lot of the major advances in technology, NOT Uncle Chuck.

> King Acorn sent Geoffrey St. John and a Secret Service team to the Floating Island to find the long-lost Queen Alicia and Prince Elias. They were successful, but Queen Alicia is on life-support, and Elias has been named the next to throne even though he doesn't want it.

> Robotnik has returned, sort of. Robo-Robotnik from Sonic #19 crossed dimensions and attacked through a giant space station. He has since reclaimed Mobotropolis and re-captured the roboticized population, except for Sonic's parents. However, his robotic body was destroyed, and he now assumes the form of Eggman from the Sonic Adventure games.

Those have been the 3 key angles that have gone on in the last year and a half. What do you think?

2 Questions

I have two questions

1. Exactly what is the difference between the chaos emeralds and the super emeralds of Floating Island?

2. What is the Little Planet?

These are the only questions I have, I'm a real hard core Sonic fan.

1) The Super Emeralds are supposed to be the rarest of all the power sources on the entire planet of Mobius. Very few, if any, are supposed to exist anymore. Based on how the comics depict them, the Super Emeralds are hundreds of times more powerful than the standard Chaos Emeralds.

2) The Little Planet was created for the Sonic CD game on the now-defunct SEGA CD platform. According to the plotline, it's a moon of sorts that appears above a place on Mobius called Never Lake for a span of a few days annually. It's also the home of the Time Stones. Robotnik tried to claim the Time Stones, and also sent Metal Sonic to capture Amy Rose. This brought Sonic onto the scene, who captured all the Time Stones, rescued Amy Rose, and restored the Little Planet to its proper state.

1. The Games Factory is made by Europress Software (www.europress.co.uk). I'm not sure if it can be bought in a store in North America, but I think it is for sale on their website.
2. The Mecha Sonics... That's a good question. It's hard to say if they were the same or not. The Silver Sonic from Sonic 2 is definitely a different robot - both Mecha and Silver appear in Sonic Adventure. However, since the 'hybrid' design from S3&K doesn't make an appearance, it's possible that the S3&K Mecha was just the normal one, but with different armor. On the other hand, the CD Mecha has made many appearances (Sonic Adventure, Knuckles' Chaotix, Sonic Triple Trouble, the racing games), and he didn't have an alternate armor in any of those games.
