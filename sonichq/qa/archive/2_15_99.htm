Espio, Sonic, and Mighty, and Where's My Fan-fic

Hi! It's me, SonIXcool again. I have a few questions. 1) Why is everyone whining about Sonic comics lately. I think they're better now than ever. At least they' center on Sonic. I mean, in issues before #47, everything was about Knuckles or Sally (one of the main reasons they gave Knux his own book.) By the way, I though ENDGAME rocked. (Warning: I am very fragile emotionally. Do not get mad at me or call me a loser. It will send me right to my therapist.) 2) Does Ken P. have plans to write a story about Espio anytime soon. Not a back up, but a story that really explores the character's personality? 3) What does "Espio" mean? 4) Why is Mighty so nice? I mean, he's the strong tough guy, but Ken P. writes him like he's just a sentimental sissy. I mean, if he's an old friend of Sonic, I'd expect him to be a little more rough around the edges. Don't get me wrong. Aside from Knux and Espio, he's my favorite character in KTE. 5) I sent in a fan-fic titled "The Mecha War." Where is it? Not that I'm impatient, I just want to know how soon it'll be up. I hope you guys don't think I'm weird. I'm just different (to quote my therapist.)

1. There are numerous reasons, but one of the biggest is that a lot of people want the Freedom Fighters to be more involved in the stories.
2. Hopefully, but there haven't been any announcements yet.
3. It's probably short for "Espionage"
4. The comics take a lot of creative license. Ken Penders probably just didn't want Mighty to be too much like Sonic. I'm not very familiar with how they're portrayed in the comic, but according to Knuckles' Chaotix, Mighty is clever, sorta reserved and quiet, and dislikes weakness. Vector's friendly and laid-back, Charmy's sophisticated and calm, and Espio is freedom-loving, sorta hot-headed, and a showoff.
5. Trooper has to read, rate, summarize, and spell-check each one, so it's not easy to get fics up quickly, especially since there are so many submitted.

P.S. - I thought your questions were great, and you should be glad to be different.
