Sonic & Tails

why is sonic so cool, and in your 'who's who?' bit, why does it say that sonic calls tails: 'little brother'? instead of: 'little buddy'?

yours questionlingly, martyn jasinski

1. Coolness is not usually something that can be described easily, and different people have different reasons to call something cool. I would point out Sonic's cool design, the unique cubist/surreal graphical style, great music, an interesting cast of characters, and the fact that you can either run through the zones or explore them in detail.
2. Because Sonic has an "older brother" relationship with Tails. I'll have to ask WB to be sure, but I think that's what Sonic calls Tails in Japan. Either that, or it was a bit of interpretation.
