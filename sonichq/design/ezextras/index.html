Unlimited Custom Posticons - updated 7-9-01
Unlimited Custom Posticons is a script that can be entered on your add topic/reply ages to provide any number of posticons you want, rather than the usual 10. The default script includes 80 posticons, and the list of posticons is totally customizable.
