Smilies !!!

Sometimes you cant really express how you feel with some words. For example - Some things may need a little "boost" to accentuate your feelings! 'Smilies' (or emoticons) are small graphical images that can be used to convey that emotion or feeling. If you have used email or internet chat, you are likely familiar with the smilie concept. Because of the limited EZBoard smilies, we havent been able to use different smilies as much as we'd like, BUT NO MORE!

This is where the "SHQ Smilie Repository" comes in. When you enter the forum, open up the repository by clicking on the link (it will open up in a separate smaller browsing window). When you need a smilie, just highlight the entire code, right click and choose "Copy" and in your message, right click again, choose "Paste" and viola - Instant smilie! REMEMBER - You have to have your EZCodes enabled though! ^_^

Special thanks to Real Tails for giving us a place to archiive these! ^__^ Have fun - and remember: Smile!
