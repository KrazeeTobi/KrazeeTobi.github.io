Box Covers and Box Art

This page includes pictures of video game box covers, the artwork they were based on (without the logos and text), and music CD covers.

The color pics were found at Knuxcom, which got most of them from Sega Online, with 2 exceptions. The Sonic Mega CD box came from Max's Sonic Zone, and the European Sonic CD (PC) box came from Magnus Anderson's Sonic The Hedgehog Page.

All of the black and white box covers were converted into .gif format by Sharpknux. They were originally encoded into a format on the Sonic Jam CD, which Remix discovered how to decode. Please give Sharpknux credit for getting these b/w pics on the net if you choose to use them.
