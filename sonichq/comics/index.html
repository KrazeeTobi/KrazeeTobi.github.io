The First Sonic Comic
Before there was Archie, before there was Egmont Fleetway, before there was the Saturday morning cartoon, - heck EVEN BEFORE Sonic The Hedgehog #1/4 the first Sonic comic, there was the 15 page promo for Sonic The Hedgehog 1. Sega Of America was planning to leave its mark on the videogaming world. However, there was only one problem - they needed a mascot. Nintendo had Mario, Atari had Pac Man, so what did Service Games use? Their answer was a little red, white, and blue Hedgehog named Sonic and his adversary and mortal nemesis - the evil Doctor Robotnik, aka Doctor Eggman! So behold, the actual seven pages from The Disney Adventures Promo. Written And Drawn By Francis Mao. Most noteably this is the story that set the basis for the entire Fleetway Sonic Universe and introduced the "Kintobor" concept used in both Archie AND Fleetway... Happy reading! A VERY BIG Thanks goes to Cristian "Conekiller" Alvernaz for sending us the lost conclusion!
