Welcome to the Sonic Images Gallery - Fleetway Comics: Produced over the seas in picturesque Great Britian, the English "Sonic The Comic" follows a decidedly different tone that its American counterpart! Based in part off of the Disney Adventures Sonic Promotional, this bi-weekly book was the first of the two Sonic comics and was published with new stories until 2000, when it went into reprints. The gallery below isn't much, but it does comprise of a lot of the early issues of Fleetway and lets you understand how it looked way back when! We'll try to add more whenever we can get them. :) Have fun browsing!
