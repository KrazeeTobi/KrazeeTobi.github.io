Promotional Art Gallery
  • The Cutie Called Amy Rose!
    Art: James Fry
    Source: Sonic #77

Little Amy takes precedence in this opening scene from Sonic 77 signaling the return of our heroes from thier space adventure! ^^ Get a good look - cause after this she'll be undergoing a few changes...

  • Sir Sonic
    Art: James Fry
    Source: Super #78

It took a long time to get here, but Sonic finally gains knighthood - and respect - from King Acorn as a hero to all to the Kingdom Of Knothole. A simple but VERY effective splash page by James Fry.

  • Signaling Adventure!
    Art: James Fry
    Source: Sonic #78

The Sonic Adventure starts here, and with it comes Chaos as we see Doctor Eggman deploying his troops, the Shodow Swatbots in this full page splash by James Fry!

  • Pro Art: Sonic #78
    Art: Harvo

A speedy Sonic flips off a power ring in a pretty cool looking picture by fan fave Harvo! Speed lines and all! ^^

  • Fall Of The Floating Island!
    Art: Steven Butler
    Source: Sonic #79

Upon the coming of Sonic Adventure, Knuckles gets the shock of his life as the Floating Island plummets into the sea in this VERY COOL double spread splash page! And if the Island falls then that means that the Master Emerald must be...

  • Sonic Adventure - B&W #1
    Art: Steven Butler
    Source: Never Released

Done in pencil, this is a never before printed piece of Artwork featuring scenes from Tikal's flashback! ^^ This piece wasn't used when certain parts of the adaption had to be cut down after the Knuckles cancellation and certain parts rewritten. Check it out and see if you can figure the sequence of events.

  • Sonic Adventure - B&W #2
    Art: Steven Butler
    Source: Sonic #80

One of the pages from Sonic 80, Robotnik discovers the secret of Chaos and just what happens when he attains Perfect form...And in an attempt to get it quicker, prepares his assualt on the Floating Island!

  • Pro Art: Sonic #80
    Art: Nelson Ortega
    Source: Sonic #80

In this rather impressive looking Pro Art piece from longtime Sonic artist Nelson Ortega, The Freedom Fighters lead the attack while Robotnik looks onto them, all the time holding the group in the palm of his hand...

  • Pro Art: Sonic #82
    Art: Sam Maxwell
    Source: Sonic #82

It may not look like much, but this piece is pretty cool, showing the classic Sonic The Hedgehog in a running pose done by Sam Maxwell! ^^

  • Pro Art: Sonic #80
    Art: Nelson Ortega
    Source: Sonic #80

The counterpart to the piece above, this pic by Sam Maxwell highlights the classic Knuckles The Echidna design getting ready for a punch! ^^

  • Pro Art: Sonic #82
    Art: Sam Maxwell & Manny Galan
    Source: Sonic #82 & Knuckles #19

This is the logo for the SHQ Pro Art page and shows just what you can do with these things if you have the time (and boredom)! ^^ Its the Knuckles pic to the left, colored and merged with a Black and White Knuckles page BG by Manny Galan.

  • Pro Art: Sonic #83
    Art: Frank Strom
    Source: Sonic #83

A pretty pic by Frank Strom, this simple piece shows Sally relaxing under a tree while Sonic prepares an ambush! ^^

  • Super Sonic Reborn !!!
    Art: Steven Butler
    Source: Sonic #83

Sonicmon, digivolve to - Super Sonicmon! ^__^ Couldn't resist. This full page splash introduces the last of the "new looks": Super Sonic - now looking a lot meaner and cooler with spiked hair, curved quills, and the trademark blood red eyes as everyone looks on in amazement at the new transformation...

  • Super Sonic VS Perfect Chaos
    Art: Steven Butler
    Source: Sonic #83

Head to head, the conclusion of Sonic Adventure begins! Super Sonic VS Perfect Chaos! And if you've played the game you know theres only one way this can end, in this cool looking full page splash... ^^

  • Pro Art: Sonic #86
    Art: Patrick Spaziante
    Source: Sonic #86

This is a COOL picture. Of course most pics by Spaz are. ^___^ This pic features ALL the Metals that have appeared in continuity: (From top to bottom) Hyper Metal Sonic, Silver Sonic #2, Silver Sonic #1, Metal Sonic #2, Mecha Sonic, Metal Sonic #1, Sonic The Hedgehog, and Psuedo Sonic ^__^ A DEFENITE keeper! ^^

  • Let's Do This
    Art: James Fry
    Source: Sonic #88

This is one of those Name The Character pics that James Fry is known for. See if you can find characters from Lodoss War, Karl Bollers, a Klingon Weapon, and other such insanity! A pretty cool splash page! ^^

  • Pro Art: Knuckles #18
    Art: Nelson Ribiero
    Source: Knuckles #18

It's Espio The Chameleon! ^^ Phasing in (or is it phasing out?) in this full color Pro Art piece by Nelson Ribiero

  • Pro Art: Knuckles #19
    Art: Mike Kazaleh
    Source: Knuckles #19

In this Pro Art piece by Mike Kazaleh (former scribe of Ren & Stimpy), Knuckles takes on a Robotnik controlled Robian!

  • Pro Art: Knuckles #20
    Art: Frank Strom
    Source: Knuckles #20

This is probably the best piece of artwork by Frank Strom I've seen - a picture of a somber Julie-Su walking along the beach! VERY cute ^^
