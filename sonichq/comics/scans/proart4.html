Promotional Art Gallery
  • Dark Legion Awaken - B&W #1
    Art: Manny Galan
    Source: Knuckles #2

A production picture of one of the page layouts for Knuckles: The Dark Legion by Manny Galan! ^^.

  • Floating Island Snowfall - B&W #2
    Art: Manny Galan
    Source: Knuckles #19

Another production promo piece, this featuring a more dramatic scene of the unprepared echidna's during a freakish blizzard!

  • Off Ya Go !!! - B&W #2
    Art: Manny Galan
    Source: Knuckles #14

The final production piece, this one from The Chaotix Caper, featuring Julie-Su getting tossed headfirst off a building! ^^

  • Pro Art: Knuckles #21
    Art: Steven Butler
    Source: Knuckles #21

Another pro art piece by Steven Butler - this time featuring Sonic and Knuckles toe to toe against a common foe: Doctor Robotnik and his Eggbot horde! ^^.

  • Full Metal Enerjak
    Art: Jim Valentino
    Source: Knuckles #22

This is a VERY intricate piece of work...This was the first time in a couple of months since we had seen what happened to Dimitri (formerly known as Enerjak), the leader and patron of the Dark Legion. And with his loss of power he's been upgraded MASSIVELY as evident in this picture with all its wires and robotics. An impressive pic by Mr. Valentino

  • Pro Art: Knuckles #22
    Art: Harvo
    Source: Knuckles #22

Knuckles and the Chaotix - courtesy of Harvo! ^^.

  • Reunion
    Art: Manny Galan
    Source: Knuckles #25

Manny Galan does a simple, but pretty effective picture - and a rarity of Knuckles, showing the softer side of him. After all the secrecy, and having not seen him for over nearly 6 years - Knuckles embraces the father who left him in this double page spread from the 25th Anniversary issue of Knuckles...

  • Pro Art: Knuckles #24
    Art: Nelson Ortega
    Source: Knuckles #24

Nelson Ortega delivers another solid pro piece! This one featuring Knuckles and Julie taking on the Dark Legion and a swarm of Mechanauts! :)

  • Pro Art: Knuckles #25
    Art: Manny Galan
    Source: Knuckles #25

Godzilla-la-la-la-la-la-la ! ^^ A seriously post apocalyptic Knuckles and a Julie-Su with bat wings takes on Godzilla in this Neo Tokyo-ish Pro Art piece by definative Knuckles artist, Manny Galan!

  • Friends No More
    Art: Art Mawhinney
    Source: Knuckles #29

This piece is significant in a number of ways - 1) Its the last piece of art by one of the original Sonic artists, Art Mawhinney, for Archie Comics. 2) It symbolizes the breakup of the childhood friendship of Knuckles and Sally 3) It marks a VERY beautiful piece of Knuckles watching Sally flying off across the ocean towards home. A definite click.

  • Pro Art: Knuckles #29
    Art: Frank Strom
    Source: Knuckles #29

The Chaotix come back in this neat looking promo piece by Frank Strom! His style gives them an almost childlike appearance which is actually kinda cool looking! Especially his Charmy Bee. ^^

  • Pro Art: Knuckles #30
    Art: Ramona Fradon
    Source: Knuckles #30

Knuckles narrowly avoids a swipe from Stage One Enerjak in this promo piece by Ramona Fradon.

  • Pro Art: Knuckles #31
    Art: Frank Strom
    Source: Knuckles #31

I'm tending to like Frank Stroms promo's more than I like some of the stories. ^^ His artwork has greatly improved from his early days and this pic is very evident of that. Knuckles makes a leap against an oncoming Super Shadow Swatbot!

  • Pro Art: Knuckles #32
    Art: Ron Lim
    Source: Knuckles #32

Ron Lim can do some pretty sweet artwork when allowed to have time with his pieces - and this one is eveident of that. Knuckles stands "looking at the camera" in this cool looking profile shot of him having taken care of the enemy (his way of course^_^)

  • Pro Art: Knuckles #28
    Art: Frank Strom
    Source: Knuckles #28

Ken Penders does this promo piece of Knuckles and Julie on a melee against The Dark Legion.

  • To Catch A Gatchaman
    Art: Nelson Ribiero
    Source: Super Special #12

This is probably one of the single funniest full page spreads in Sonic I've seen! Anime fans will DEFINETELY wanna check this out! ^^ Sonic, Amy, Tails, Knuckles, and Rotor each portray a role of the well known Japanese cartoon: Gatchaman (known in America as G-Force and Battle Of The Planets!) Now if only we could get a DBZ riff in there... ^^

  • Sonic Underground
    Art: Jim Valentino
    Source: Super Special #11

Its thier only appearance in comic continuity, but fans of Sonic Underground were treated to this full page spread of Sonic, Manic and Sonia in concert, courtesy of Jim Valentino! ^^

  • Photo Finish
    Art: Nelson Ribiero & Patrick Spaziante
    Source: Super Special #9

This is a cute picture. ^^ Sonic looks in a photo album highling specific moments of continuity past! Knuckles meets Sally, a certain "Doctor" named Warlord Julian discusses the future with King Acorn, and Sonic "snows off" at Antione! ^__^

  • Pro Art: Super Special #11
    Art: Ron Lim
    Source: Super Special #11

This piece kicks @$$! ^__^ Looking at it from the sides and from the regular view shows two different perspectives! VERY cool!
