Crossovers
Archie & Friends "A Halloween Tale" - October, 1998

"A Festival of Fantasy"
Writer(s): Ken Penders
Penciler(s): Dan DeCarlo & Patrick Spaziante
Inker(s): Ken Penders

This free comic was a Halloween giveaway. Our stars Sonic and Knuckles plus Sabrina and all the Archie comics characters appear in this comical 12-page mini-comic.

Cover: Patrick Spaziante

CSN #619-#632 - May-July, 1999

"Turnabout Heroes"
Writer(s): Karl Bollers & Ken Penders
Penciler(s): James Fry
Inker(s): Andrew Pepoy

CSN, a free weekly fanzine, will be printing a Sonic and Knuckles strip for fourteen weeks straight with the first installment starting in the first week of May. Though it is a way to introduce new readers to our favorite characters, current readers will want to get it themselves since it will be placed in continuity (specifically between Sonic #73 & #74 and between Knuckles #25 & #26). The story: What to Sonic & Knuckles do when they wake up... in each other's body? They create mayhem, what else?

Cover: Patrick Spaziante & Harvey Mercadoocasio

Sabrina the Teenage Witch #28 - June, 1999

"Some Enchantra Evening"
Writer(s): Mike Gallagher
Penciler(s): Dan DeCarlo & Dave Manak
Inker(s): Jon D'Agostino

This issue features the first part of a two-part Sabrina-Sonic crossover story. The first part of the story begins with a dispute between Enchantra, queen of the Other Realm, and Sabrina. That's why Enchantra uses her powers to bring Sonic from his homeworld of Mobius to Sabrina's hometown of Westbridge... and to lead him to attack Sabrina! The story continues in Sonic Super Special #10, which reveals that Savrina has more than Sonic to worry about: Enchantra has left the portal to Mobius open, which means that some of Sonic's villainous enemes could find their way into Westbridge!

Cover: Dan DeCarlo, Patrick Spaziante, & Jon D'Agostino
