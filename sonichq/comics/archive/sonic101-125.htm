Sonic the Hedgehog #101-125
Sonic The Hedgehog #101 - September, 2001

"Altered States"
Writers: Karl Bollers & Ken Penders
Pencilers: Ron Lim & Dawn Best
Inkers: Pam Eklund & Andrew Pepoy

After the catastrophic events of Sonic #100, Sonic and his friends find themselves living in a world that bears no resemblance to the one they've always known. Who - or - what is responsible? Plus: Knuckles in "Reboot." Can it be so? Has Knuckles aligned himself with Dimitri and the Dark Legion? And can he really be serious about reversing history without considering the repercussions? Could it possibly tie into the intrigue Sonic and his friends have recently found themselves in?

Cover: Patrick Spaziante & Nelson Ribeiro

Sonic The Hedgehog #102 - October, 2001

"Family Dysfunction"
"Life's Realities"
Writers: Karl Bollers & Ken Penders
Pencilers: Ron Lim & Dawn Best
Inkers: Andrew Pepoy & Pam Eklund

There's much cause for celebration when Sonic, Tails, Princess Sally and the Freedom Fighters return to Knothole with the roboticized mobians. So, if that's the case, why isn't the Royal Family smiling? Maybe it's because they've been betrayed by trusted allies, and are being held hostage in Castle Acorn! Can Sonic save them all? Fans won't believe how this issue ends! The thrilling conclusion to last issue's story wherein Knuckles has only two more chances to change history, hopefully resulting in a utopia where all echidnas get along and Mobius is a paradise..but will Knuckles spot the one flaw in his plan in time to correct it?

Cover: Patrick Spaziante & Nelson Ribeiro
