Sonic the Hedgehog #101-125
Sonic The Hedgehog #101 - September, 2001

"Altered States"

Writer: Karl Bollers
Penciler: Ron Lim
Inkers: Pam Eklund & Andrew Pepoy

After the catastrophic events of Sonic #100, Sonic and his friends find themselves living in a world that bears no resemblance to the one they've always known. Who - or - what is responsible?

"Reboot."

Writer: Ken Penders
Penciler: Dawn Best
Inker: Andrew Pepoy

Can it be so? Has Knuckles aligned himself with Dimitri and the Dark Legion? And can he really be serious about reversing history without considering the repercussions? Could it possibly tie into the intrigue Sonic and his friends have recently found themselves in?

Cover: Patrick Spaziante & Nelson Ribeiro

Sonic The Hedgehog #102 - October, 2001

"Family Dysfunction"

Writer: Karl Bollers
Penciler: Ron Lim
Inkers: Andrew Pepoy & Pam Eklund

There's much cause for celebration when Sonic, Tails, Princess Sally and the Freedom Fighters return to Knothole with the roboticized mobians. So, if that's the case, why isn't the Royal Family smiling? Maybe it's because they've been betrayed by trusted allies, and are being held hostage in Castle Acorn! Can Sonic save them all? Fans won't believe how this issue ends!

"Life's Realities"

Writer: Ken Penders
Penciler: Dawn Best
Inker: Pam Eklund

The thrilling conclusion to last issue's story wherein Knuckles has only two more chances to change history, hopefully resulting in a utopia where all echidnas get along and Mobius is a paradise..but will Knuckles spot the one flaw in his plan in time to correct it?

Cover: Patrick Spaziante & Nelson Ribeiro

Sonic The Hedgehog #103 - November, 2001

"Freedom Fighters of the Galaxy, Part One"

Writer: Michael Gallagher
Penciler: Jim Valentino
Inker: Harvey Mercadoocasio

A tribute to the Guardians of the Galaxy brought to readers by the team that created them, writer Mike Gallagher and artist Jim Valentino. In an alternate future, Sonic comes back to Mobius after being in space for a thousand years to find the planet in worse shape than when he left it. Teaming up with the descendants of the original Freedom Fighters, the heroes combat an evil so great, they with the original Robotnik was back.

"Myth Taken Identity, Part One"

Writer: Michael Gallagher
Penciler: Dave Manak
Inker: Harvey Mercadoocasio

The Return of Downunda Freedom Fighters, squaring off against the Bunyip in a tale recalling the early days of the Sonic comic series.

Cover: Patrick Spaziante & Harvey Mercadoocasio

Sonic The Hedgehog #104 - December, 2001

"Freedom Fighters of the Galaxy, Part Two"

Writer: Michael Gallagher
Penciler: Dawn Best
Inker: Ken Penders

The tribute to the Guardians of the Galaxy continues. Sonic's troubles in the alternate future version of Mobius go from bad to worse, as the harbinger of doom arrives - and his name is Silver Snively. That can only mean on thing: his master, the incarnation of doom itself, can't be far behind - none other than Robolactus. Can Sonic and the future Freedom Fighters stop this menace before he has a chance to erase them from the history books.

"Myth Taken Identity, Part Two"

Writer: Michael Gallagher
Penciler: Dave Manak
Inker: Harvey Mercadoocasio

A continuation of the confrontation begun last issue, as the Downunda Freedom Fighters square off against Bunyip in a classic-style Sonic tale.

Cover: Patrick Spaziante & Ken Penders
