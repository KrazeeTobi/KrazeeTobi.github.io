How the award works...

 
Okay, now, if you'd like to win this award, your site must have a couple features that we at Sonic HQ are looking for. First off, the award name is Sonic Oscar Award and that is copyrighted © 1999 to Sonic HQ. If you receive an award you will receive an image through e-mail as an attatchment, this will verify that you've won. Along with you having the image on your site, a link to your site will be posted on a page here at Sonic HQ. This award will be given every month to a different site, you cannot win this more than once! Now, on to the specifications:
  • Your site MUST be Sonic related. Whether it being all about Tails, Knuckles, SatAM, etc., it can be anything related to Sonic. It can be games, comics, television, doesn't matter.
  • Your site MUST consist of more than 1 page. It must be a chain of pages.
  • Your site should have a decent looking layout with images. It doesn't have to be professional, but it should look appealing to the eye of the viewers.
  • When submitting, you must write all the correct information down; please do not use aliases for the name, make sure you have everything correct.
  • Your explanation why your site should win must consist of at least 2 sentences. We're not too picky, so don't hesitate to express yourself!
  • Last but not least, your site must be original, and did not take information from another site and just copy/paste it onto yours.
  • If your site meets all these requirements, then it's on to the form for you! Good Luck!
  • Oh, and one more thing - if you don't win, you won't recieve a notice saying so. I just can't handle emailing people telling them 'YOU LOSE.' ^_^ I realise this kind of leaves you holding the bag, but as the award is given out on a monthly basis, the winner will always be notified by the end of the month. So, if the first of the month rolls around and you haven't recieved anything, well, better luck next time. ;D
