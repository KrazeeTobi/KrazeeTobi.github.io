Sonic News Press

Welcome to the SHQ News Archives. Let me tell you how it works. ^_^

Whenever an older article is pushed off the page, or a new month comes into play, the article will automatically be stored here in the archive section for permanent keeping. Each link holds _every_ article for every month all the way through the beginning of the Sonic HQ News page.

Because some months were VERY large news months, that does mean that some archives may take a bit to load considering just how much news was reported for that month. But otherwise - EVERYTHINGS here. ^__^ Have fun looking.
