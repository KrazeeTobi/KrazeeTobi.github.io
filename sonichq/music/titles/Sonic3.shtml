Sonic the Hedgehog 3
Released on "Hedgehog Day," February 2, 1994, Sonic 3 introduced Knuckles and refined the gameplay of the Sonic series by giving different abilities to different characters. Those who used the Level Select / Debug code discovered music and zone names that weren't used in the main game, as well as inaccessible areas in some acts. These lost areas and zones were later restored by Sonic & Knuckles and the unique Lock-On Technology.
