Sonic The Hedgehog

Full Name: Sonic Maurice Hedgehog
Nick Names: The Blue Blur, True Blue
Year Of Birth: 3219
Age: 16 years
Sex: Male
Species: Blue Hedgehog
Place Of Residence: Knothole Village, Mobotropolis
Abilities: Super Speed, Spin Dash Attack, Figure Eight Move, Super Peel-Out Move, Whirlwind Attack
Weapons: Power Rings, Chaos Emeralds, The Cyclone - Race Car (Sonic Drift 1 and 2)
Known Relatives: Uncle: Sir Charles Hedgehog (Uncle Chuck), Father: Jules The Hedgehog, Mother: Bernie The Hedgehog, Uncle: Sir Charles "Chuck" Hedgehog
Weakness: Very very impatient, likes to jump into things without using his brain first.
First Appearance: Sonic #0 And Sonic The Hedgehog 1

Raised in New Mobotropolis before Robotnik's coup, Sonic was an average growing boy, exception being his insane speed which he worked on improving everyday. Sonic lost his parents to the roboticiser in The Great War, and was taken in by his Uncle Chuck, whom he lived with in Knothole, until he too was roboticised. Sonic was then raised in Mobotropolis by his Uncle before Robotnik's coup. Helpless to stop any of the going ons as Robotnik took control of the populace, roboticized most of the Mobians, and took over the planet, Sonic and a few other children, including the Princess, were herded to Knothole Villiage where they could learn, grow, and adapt to situations and eventually become a freedom fighting force that could one day defeat Robotnik and free Mobius. Currently, Sonic, along with Tails, have left the Freedom Fighters temporarily to track down the whereabouts of the wizard Ixis Naugus. When Sonic collects seven Chaos Emeralds, he becomes Super Sonic. As Super Sonic, all of his natural abilities are enhanced! His speed is so incredible that he actually hovers a few feet above the ground rather than running on it. He is completely invincible to all attacks (except getting smashed or drowning). His color also changes from brilliant blue to shining yellow. However, he needs atleast fifty Rings to utilize Chaos Emerald power, and he must keep a steady supply or he'll turn back to normal Sonic.

Not much is known of Tails' background. Tails seemed to have been abondened as a little kid, but Sonic found him and brought him into their family of Freedom Fighters when he was 5 years old. Since then, he has grown older, now being able to go on many missions with the main group. His two tails has been of invaluable assistance to the Freedom Fighters as well. He often refers to Sally as Aunt Sally and thinks of Sonic as his big brother as well as a sort of father. Tails view Sonic as the only hero in the universe, and will always try to be exactly like him. Tails also is the Chosen One, as destinied by the Ancient Walkers, to be the greatest hero of his time. Tails is outgoing and is the kind of person who can make a friend out of anybody, except maybe Doc Robotnik. He's also committed to his cause and gets very determined to do his job when he takes part in missions, resulting in an extreme bravery. When Tails collects seven Chaos Emeralds, he becomes Turbo Tails. As Turbo Tails, all of his natural abilities are enhanced. He is also invincible to all attacks (except getting smashed or drowning), and glows a bright orange-yellow color. Four Super Flicky birds come to his aid, destroying every Badnik in sight and clearing a path for him. However, he needs atleast fifty Rings to utilize Chaos Emerald power, and he must keep a steady supply or he'll turn back to normal Tails.

He devestated everything and then her father disappeared. Since then, Sally has been living in Knothole Villiage as the the leader of the Freedom Fighters, determined to defeat Robotnik and win back her rightful heritage as ruler of the soon to be free Mobius. Heroic, intelligent, and very wise in modern situations as well as war tactics. She's more than your normal princess as she studied immensly under the tutelage of her royal teacher, Julayla after the King vanished. She is serious about her work and is committed to her cause. However, she is annoyed by Sonic's attitude as well, but deep down inside, she knows she loves Sonic and he loves her. Neither of them will admit it though unless something really forces them to. Currently her father has been found and the person she once found herself fighting for - she has become more distant from....namely her father King Acorn!

Rob O' The Hedge comes from the area of the planet known as Mercia where he was the leader of The Krazy Kritter Freedom Fighters....until they were roboticised. It was in his country where Robotnik sent his underboss - General D'Coolette ( there known as The Highsheriff ). Like all the other underbosses The Highsherriff began his reign of terror by roboticising the public and forcing others into hiding in Deerwood Forest much like the Freedom Fighters of today. Setting up the rebellion in Mercia, Rob began to fight the good fight to keep his country from falling under the sway of The Highsheriff. Fearing the life of his cousin - Amy Rose - he sent her away to Knothole Village where he hoped that she would be able to avoid the war and its harsh effects....It wasnt until years later that Sonic and Knuckles discovered Rob. Though Robotnik had been defeated at the time, The Highsheriff still held sway in Mercia. Knuckles at the time - helping out The Forgotten Tribe and Sonic on the trail of Ixis Naugus agreed to help him in liberating his kingdom. By this time his entire Freedom Fighter group had been roboticised and he was the only one left along the last few people there. It was also by him that Sonic found out more info on Amy Rose seeing as how little to none knew of her past as well as learning another secret - The Highsherriff was Antione's father! Once thought a casualty to the war in truth he had been roboticised and lost of all free will! It was at this time that Rob met Mari-An - one of the members of the Lost tribe and they fell in love. After Sonic and Knux helped him destroy The Highsherriff's castle, Rob aided Knux in finding the lost city of Albion at Journey's End, as Mari-An decided to stay with the man she had fallen in love with....Currently the two are in Mercia cleaning up what is left of the castle and trying to figure out a way to deroboticise the Mercian FF's...
