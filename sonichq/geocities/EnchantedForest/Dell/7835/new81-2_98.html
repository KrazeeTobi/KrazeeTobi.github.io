This is the Sonic news page archive for August 1-19th, 1998. Oh, and meet the News Crew!

8-19-98 They Are Real!
 The Sonic Adventure screen shots we showed you yesterday are real! But very early versions of the game. Sega has requested that all sites take them down until (in other words, if the links break down there, it's not our fault), 8-22-98, this Friday. Expect the final showing this weekend to be way better than these screens! Also, remember the Sonic Adventure commercial we reported a while ago? Some of it are from the actual game! Cool, huh? The commercial stood for Sonic coming and creating all the chaos in the streets.

8-19-98 Manny Galan...
 Lien-Na reporting: He is a really fast artist, Knuckles is ahead 5 issues. It would have been 7 but he said Ken Penders had trouble keeping up with him! Aside from 23 pages of Knuckles a month he does the occasional Sonic back-up, 10-14 pages of Scooby Doo every so often, advertisements every so often, and now works a full time job with Nickelodeon. After #25 he'll only be doing back-up stories for a while because he needs to do work that is going to pay him more and allow more free time. But he says not to worry though because he will be working on what he hopes to be his finest work ever, 2 issues of Knuckles that he wrote for fun, which should be scheduled some time in the 30th issues.

8-19-98 The Man On the Cover...
 Asudem reporting: The guy on the bottom of the Sonic Super Special #7 cover is named Dr. Eandroid. He's one of the Lost Ones (Ken Pender's creation, another comic book that's due out a few month from now, go to his page for more info). Dr. Eandroid is after the Master Emerald...his special ability (all Lost Ones have a special ability) is to become something. What I mean is that he can build himself. He can transport his mind into other objects. He CAN'T transport his mind into living creatures. Once his mind is transported into something inanimate, he controls whatever his mind went into. So, that means he can build himself! All he has to do is get a non-living body and use it to make a robot for himself! Once he makes a robot body for himself, he can transport his mind into it and control it.

8-18-98 Sonic Underground pushed back to Next Season!?
 At a fur party that night, I met someone from DIC who is working on Sonic Underground. This is not a rumor. The first episode is in the can, and the rest are in animation in China. Sonic and Robotnik are the only two characters returning, no Tail, Sally, Bunnie and the others. It is set for release into Syndication March of 99. What's really interesting is it's computer inked. The cels are hand drawn then scanned into a computerand colored.

This was a post from Silverfox from the San Diego Comic Con on the Sonic the Hedgehog mailing list. If what he says is true then this means that if the first episode is in the can then it's being redone. We still don't have that much new information and as far as we know production has been moved up one year. This is possibly for the better (imagine a rehash of the Adventures Of Sonic The Hedgheog.....shudder). We still have none of the previous characters but check here later for more info as we get it. Stay tuned and look for your fall previews on the TV to see if this is truth......

8-11-98 Australian's Luck Runs Out?
 About the rumor that was reported yesterday, here's what FGNonline has to say about it:

Rumors of a November Dreamcast launch in Australia have been branded a load of kanga-hooie by our sources. National radio reports in Australia had suggested that Sega would test the launch before Christmas. But magazine editors have emailed us to set the record straight. One commented: "This just isn't the case. I think someone here has got confused again. The same thing happened with the release of Cruisin' World, which was supposed to come here first but still hasn't arrived." He added: "I wish it were true, but given Sega's set-up here, it just doesn't seem possible."

Well then, guess the Dreamcast won't be released down under this November...

8-11-98 Ken Penders On San Diego Comic-Con.
 Ken's already on his way to San Diego, but this is what he said before he left:

As far as I'm aware of, the following will be attending San Diego, though not necessarily hanging out in Artists Alley as I'll be doing:

Rich Koslowski, Harvey Mercadoocasio, Andrew Pepoy, Jim Amash, Jeff Powell, Scott Shaw!, myself and Justin Gabrie, who'll probably be hanging out around my table on Sunday, August 16.

In addition, my significant other Bernie is representing Pat Spaziante and Manny Galan as she'll be offering for sale select pages and covers ranging from $50 to $150. Pat has personally autographed a number of covers, along with two of the frontispieces from the KNUCKLES: DARK VENGEANCE storyline. (Julie-Su and Enerjak, both extremely suitable for framing.)

Well, the show starts this Wednesday, so make your plans quick!
