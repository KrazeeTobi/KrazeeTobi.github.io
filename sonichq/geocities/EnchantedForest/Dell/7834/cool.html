11-02-98
Dear Sonic HQ,
I'm curious ... how exactly did Knuckles's ancestors dissapear? NR
Net Raptor

According to the comic - Knux's ancestors had been missing for over a century due to the fact of a nuclear holocaust incited bty The Dingo Armada. As oncoming missiles were heading for the island the guardian at that time - Hawking - used his Hyper Zone Projector to literally transport Echidnaopolis - located in the center of the Marble Garden Zone (Sonic 3) - , Dingo City and most animal life into sevaral "Pocket Zones" - similar to the zones we play in videogames. It was after they were transported to thier "zones" that the Island became a nuclear wasteland and it took over a century for the flora and fauna to grow back. During that time it was up to Hawking and Tobor, who were guardians during that time to get busy to restoring the Island. Over time ( Due to Haven's secrecy ) guardians were left out of the loop on this information and were left to defend the actual island itself ( after it had grown back ) with no knowledge of where thier people were - Athair, possibly Locke - BEFORE he found haven, and Knuckles. This explains why Knuckles had NO REASON of knowing what had happened to his ancestors (Sonic 3) - because all this time it had been hidden from him by those in authority (Haven). The thing that opened up the Echidnaopolis Zone and the Dingo City Zone, was the omega wave that was released when the Ultimate Annihilator exploded ( SSS6: Sonic 50 ) after that each zone was literally fighting itself for space but only one could exist. The rest is explained in "Knuckles: Lost Paradise".
