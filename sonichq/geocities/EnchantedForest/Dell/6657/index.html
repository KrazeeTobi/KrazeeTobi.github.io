Sonic HQ - The Games Page

The year is 1990. After moderate success with the Master System, Service Games (Known as SEGA) has just released the first 16-bit platform. Unbeknownst to them at the time, this 16-bit machine, known as "Mega Drive" in Asia and Europe and "Genesis" in the Americas, was destined to become a household name, the top-selling system of all time, the eighth wonder of the world, and quite literally the console that changed the way the public viewed videogames forever. This entire change of fate was the sole responsibility of three men...and one blue hedgehog. Little did they know the speedy phenomenon they would unleash on the populace....
