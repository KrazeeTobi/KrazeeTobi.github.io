Top Ten Pages

This is the Top Ten Sonic Page in our opinion as of August, 1998.

10. Tyro's Sonic The Hedgehog Info Page
A very good Sonic infomation source. A must visit if you're a new fan.

9. Tolwyn Net
Very nicely designed page. Not much variation in the pages, but still a good place to visit.

8. The Sonic Zone
A good place to visit. You can also join the Sonic Zone Newsletter.

7. Max's Sonic Zone
A really large archive of sonic stuff, including all the cover scans.

6. Virtual Knothole Village
The only virtual Sonic fan page, a must visit!

5. The Sonic Archive
One of the largest Sonic file archives. You should check it out!

4. The Sonic Fundation
Updated often, a pretty good Sonic news site. Lots of music.

3. KnuxCom
A very resourceful site, perfect if you don't know much about Sonic.

2. Artail Productions
Excellent site, with all the Sonic downloads you'll ever want.

1. Sonic HQ
Think about it, we've got the best News Page, Midi Page, Game Info Page, Q&A Page, one of the best Comic Info Page, Cartoon Info Page and much much more! Hey, we think we're the best,#:-) don't you?
