Sonic Gameplay - The Master List....

The majority of the Sonic games--the ones the ones we cover at Sonic HQ--are side-scrolling action games, A.K.A platform games. The biggest difference between Sonic and any other platform game is the fact that you can usually play with multiple characters, each with thier own storyline or controls, and you also usually acquire one or more weapons or necessary items in each of these levels - almost in an VERY LOOSE RPG fashion of sort, which adds some strategy to the way in which you play.
