Tengu Man's Stage (Saturn Version)

Enemy Mine....

Robotnik doesn't just rely on his colossus mechs and well designed deathtraps alone to get rid of Sonic and company! To help him out, he's got the help of his special created badnik armada! Whie some have gotten redesigned, most have retained that simple shape and gotten upgrades over the various games. Scattered across the endless zones as patrol, Badniks are the various enemies that Sonic will encounter in all of the Sonic games! Better known by fans as "The Badnik Army" each one is easy to defeat alone, but some have advantages over others that make them much more harder to defeat...As the games progress the badniks get a little harder and harder to defeat with each game as thier designs got more intricate...

This page is split into 3 sections - Troop A (Sonic 1, Sonic CD, and Sonic 2), Troop B (Sonic 3 & Knuckles), and Troop C (Sonic Adventure)! On each page you will find the list of the various badniks covering the games of Sonic 1, Sonic CD, Sonic 2, Sonic 3 & Knuckles, Sonic Adventure, and any other thing that we can find! Hopefully this will allow you to get acquanted with the enemies Sonic encounters much more! Look for the popular themed badniks as well - such as the Caterkiller series (which have been in near EVERY Sonic game), the popular badniks - those that made it to the TV screen in cartoons or otherwise...
