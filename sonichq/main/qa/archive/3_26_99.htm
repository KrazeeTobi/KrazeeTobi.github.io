Timing Question

Hi! I want to ask a question about the water zones in Sonic 1, 2, & 3. In each of the games, how long can Sonic last underwater without breathing?
Thanks.

30 seconds. It takes 5 seconds for the first "ding" warning, and the warning repeats at 10 seconds and 15 seconds. After 18 seconds (or 3 seconds after the 3rd warning), the "danger" music and countdown start. The countdown lasts 12 seconds.

Sonic 3 Shields

Hi! I've read the manual for Sonic 3, and I found a tip that says "Beware of badniks with energy weapons". If you have a water, flame, or lightning shield can these attacks still hurt Sonic or Tails? Thanks.

Kyle Wightman

It depends on the weapon. The Blastoids in the Carnival Night Zone shoot lightning that (I think) can be absorbed by a lightning shield, but not a water shield. The ice crystals surrounding the Star Pointers in the Ice Cap Zone will be blocked by any shield. Although they aren't really bandiks, there are plenty of objects that use fire as a weapon, and these attacks can all be blocked with a fire shield. I can't think of any other badnik energy attacks in Sonic 3, but all 3 shields reflect most small non-energy projectiles.

SA Music

How much would the Sonic Adventure music CD cost?

Thanks to Ray for this info-

Game Music Online has the "Songs With Attitude" CD for $28.95. This is the CD with the character themes, not the "Sonic Adventure Remixes" CD.

A 3rd collection of music, the "Sonic Adventure Original Soundtrack," is a 2-CD set with background music from SA, and it includes 69 tracks. As far as we know, it's only available in Japan now, and we don't even know the Japanese price, so a price would be tough to estimate.
