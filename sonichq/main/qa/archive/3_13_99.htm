Time Stones

Hi! I am sort of having troubles getting all 7 time stones in Sonic CD. How can you get them all easily?

Kyle Wightman

If you get a low enough total time in time attack (Debug will help you get it lower if you have trouble - see Game Info for how to get a 0'00" time), an extra time attack menu will be available by pressing Left while viewing the regular time attack menu. On this menu, you can play the Special Zone Stages without a time limit. This will help you to practice your Special Zone skills.

Now, when you play the regular game, don't save unless you are successful in getting 2 time stones in each area (2 in Palmtree Panic, 2 in Collision Chaos, etc.). If you are, save the game after that area is finished. If you are unsuccessful, just go back to the previously saved game. This will let you try each area over again.
