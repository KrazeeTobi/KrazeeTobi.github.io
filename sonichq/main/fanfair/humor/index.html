Mascot Wars! - updated 7-16-00 - by WB
Super Sonic VS Super Mario. Who's better? It's a question that has racked the brains of gamers since day one. Will this feud of the video game mascots EVER end or is it doomed to drag on and on? Sure you can argue and argue about those two till the end of time time itself! But lets look at it through a broader scope....Lets take a looksie as we compare Sonic and Dr. Eggman to the likes such well known mascots as Lara Croft, Crash Bandicoot and Neo Cortex, Mega Man and Doctor Wily, Pikachu And Team Rocket, Mickey Mouse, Pegleg Pete and the Phantom Blot, Donald Duck and Uncle Scrooge, Cloud Strife and Sephiroth, and MANY others you may NOT expect as we end it on Super Mario and Bowser themselves !!!! And by the way....look out!! Rumor has it there's a stray Pac-Man running around in there as well...You know how those things like to get their chomp on...
