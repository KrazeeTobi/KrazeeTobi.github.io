Knuckles the Echidna #26-32
Knuckles the Echidna #26 - May, 1999

"The First Date - She Loves You... (and you know that can't be bad!)"
Writer(s): Ken Penders
Penciler(s): Chris Allan
Inker(s): Andrew Pepoy

In a story called "The First Date", Knuckles agonizes over his feelings for Julie-Su. This story sends him on a journey that makes all the adventure and intrigue in recent issues seem like a walk in the park. Witness this romantic comedy as brought to you by Ken Penders, Chriss Allan, and Andrew Pepoy. Also, a Mighty The Armadillo solo story.

Cover: Manny Galan & Ken Penders

Knuckles the Echidna #27 - June, 1999

"The First Date - Who Wrote the Book of Love Anyway?"
Writer(s): Ken Penders
Penciler(s): Chris Allan
Inker(s): Andrew Pepoy

He's faced some of the most dangerous villains the Floating Island has ever seen, but can even Knuckles surmount the hardest task he's ever faced: figuring out how to ask Julie-Su out on a date? It's the most heart-wrenching, gut-churning chapter yet in the life of everyone's favorite (lovesick) echidna! Also, Part 2 of the Mighty solo story.

Cover: Manny Galan & Ken Penders

Knuckles the Echidna #28 - July, 1999

"The First Date - It Could Be Love (I Should Have Known Better)"
Writer(s): Ken Penders
Penciler(s): Chris Allan
Inker(s): Andrew Pepoy

Now that Knuckles has asked Julie-Su on a date, he realizes that an even greater problem exists: What will they do on a date? He becomes so absorbed with his dilemma, he completely forgets about his upcoming 16th birthday! Also, the conclusion to the Mighty solo story by Penders, Galan, and Pepoy.

Cover: Manny Galan & Ken Penders

Knuckles the Echidna #29 - August, 1999

"My Special Friend"
Writer(s): Ken Penders
Penciler(s): Art Mawhinney
Inker(s): Rich Koslowski

Guest-starring the ever-popular Princess Sally! Sally pays a visit to The Brotherhood of Guardians on The Floating Island on a diplomatic mission on behalf of her father. Will her mission of mercy succeed or fail? Don't miss this issue-length epic!

Cover: Manny Galan & Ken Penders

Knuckles the Echidna #30 - September, 1999

"King of the Hill - Bad to the Bone"
Writer(s): Ken Penders
Penciler(s): Ken Penders
Inker(s): Harvey Mercadoocasio

Knuckles faces off with his childhood rival, Monk, in a battle bringing a lifetime of grievances to bear! Of course, things go from bad to wrose as an overlander named Hunter enters the scene. Also: Espio the Chameleon, Part One by Penders with art by Collen Doran and Andrew Pepoy.

Cover: Manny Galan & Ken Penders

Knuckles the Echidna #31 - October, 1999

"King of the Hill - The Thrill of the Hunt"
Writer(s): Ken Penders
Penciler(s): Ken Penders
Inker(s): Harvey Mercadoocasio

Continuing the epic storyline, "King of the Hill" Knuckles and Monk are confronted by Hunter, an overlander who follows a life much like his namesake. Looking at them as his greatest challenge to date, he targets them for his latest hunt. Also: Espio The Chameleon, Part Two by Penders with art by Colleen Foran and Andrew Pepoy.

Cover: Manny Galan & Ken Penders

Knuckles the Echidna #32 - November, 1999

"King of the Hill - To the Death"
Writer(s): Ken Penders
Penciler(s): Ken Penders
Inker(s): Harvey Mercadoocasio

This is it! The conclusion of the current Knuckles toryline "King of the Hill!" Hunter closes in on our pair, Knuckles and Monk, as they strugle to work together for their very lives. To find out what happens, you must read this issue. Also: the conclusion fo "Espio The Chameleon" solo story by Penders with art by Collen doran and Andrew Pepoy.

Cover: Manny Galan & Ken Penders
