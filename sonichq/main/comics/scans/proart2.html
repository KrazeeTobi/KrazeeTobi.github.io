Promotional Art Gallery
  • Pro Art: Sonic #65
    Art: James Fry

The first piece of promo art by current Sonic artist James Fry showcases Sonic and Tails going at it with Doctor Robotnik in a flying saucer! While Tails dodges lasers, Sonic does his thing ^^ A pretty cool picture!

  • Pro Art: Sonic #66
    Art: Chris Allan

A promo piece by well known Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles artist Chris Allan, showcases Sonic fighting for his life against assailant Robians, both of whom (including Sonic) are falling off a cliff into the valley below!

  • The Thousand Ring Power Up!
    Art: Steven Butler
    Source: Sonic #66

The concluding battle of the Mobius World Tour: As the smoke clears - Ultra Sonic! Hyper Tails! And a Super Ixis Naugus prepare for the definative battle after having absorbed over 1000 power rings! This is a pretty cool double page spread showcasing Sonic and Tails in thier second evolution stages after first stage Super Sonic courtesy of Steven Butler. >;)

  • The Image Teaser! - Picture #1
    Art: Sam Maxwell
    Source: Sonic #66

Drawn by Sam Maxwell, this full color picture advert showcases all the Image Comics characters and the Archie Sonic characters for the crossover special in SSS7!

  • The Image Teaser - Picture #2
    Art: Ken Penders
    Source: Super Special #7

A black and white promo piece by Ken Penders, this picture lines up the Archie's VS The Image's as they prepare for the climax.

  • See The Light...
    Art: Ken Penders
    Source: Super Special #7

The Freedom Fighters make thier first impressions with the mysterious particle in this B&W promo from SSS7.

  • Down and Out Knuckles
    Art: Ken Penders
    Source: Super Special #7

Knuckles makes first contact with Particle, unfortunately SHE makes first contact with a certain Master Emerald...

  • Happy 16th Birthday, Sonic The Hedgehog!
    Art: Steven Butler
    Source: Sonic #68

Happy 16th Birthday Sonic! Preparing for his jump in age for Sonic Adventure, the hedgehog gets another year celebrated by his friends in Knothole Village for this full page spread!

  • Pro Art: Sonic #68
    Art: Ramona Fradon
    Source: Snic #68

A promo pice by the former syndicated Brenda Starr comic strip artist, Ramona Fradon starring Sonic and Robotnik.

  • Pro Art: Sonic #69
    Art: Colleen Doran
    Source: Sonic #69

Well known Wonder Woman artist Colleen Doran takes a stab at Sonic in this pinup with Sonic and Tails against a pretty sky background! Too bad its B&W.

  • Pro Art: Sonic #70
    Art: David Brewer
    Source: Sonic #70

Having finished off the enemy with ease, Sonic strikes a pose while his robotic conquest smolders in the background.

  • Saving Nate Morgan - Opening Panel
    Art: Steven Butler
    Source: Sonic #70

The opening panel of the Sonic 71 story was shortened by half a page probably due to printing needs. Both of them are pretty impressive considering thier perspectives. This one features Sonic and The Freedom Fighters after having been ambushed with a bombing in Deer John's Juice Joint by the Prison Escapees from the Devil's Gulag. Definetely a looker! ^^

  • Look For Adventure!
    Art: Steven Butler
    Source: Sonic #71

The adventure has arrived - Sonic Adventure that is! In further preparation for the upcoming Sonic Adventure adaption, Sonic takes on his new look complete with Green Eyes, new shoes, and longer quills in this "first look" splash page!

  • Pro Art: Sonic #72
    Art: Suzanne Paddock
    Source: Sonic #72

Newcomer to the book, Suzanne Paddock renders a very pretty looking Sally against Knothole Village, shown in full from its SatAM design. ^^

  • He's Baaaaaaaack...!
    Art: Steven Butler
    Source: Sonic #72

The full page splash that sent shockwaves. Not seen since Endgame, Doc R's back in business! And Snively, being exposed for having "killed" his uncle, just pretty much had a heartattack. ^__^

  • "Knothole's Gone."
    Art: Steven Butler
    Source: Sonic #74

The Robian inhabitants of Knothole Village - including Sonic's mother, father, and uncle - have vanished into thin air. And Sonic, having realized that his hopes for a happy family are over, can only state the obvious against a pretty cool black art deco background in this full page splash from Sonic 74.

  • Pro Art: Sonic #74
    Art: Gordon Purcell
    Source: Sonic #74

Sonic, Geoffrey, and Knuckles take on some attacking Robians in this pro art pice by newcomer Gordon Purcell.

  • Pro Art: Sonic #76
    Art: Pam Eklund
    Source: Sonic #76

Now THIS piece kicks butt ^^! Sonic and Knuckles inker Pam Eklund etches (probably on a scratchboard) a picture of Sonic and Sally exploring a subterranean cave. The lighting and shadow are superb in this piece and I highly recommend giving it a click. ^^
