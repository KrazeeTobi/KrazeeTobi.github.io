September 12, 2000

Episode Of The Day! - Because I like the idea so much, I've decided to keep the "Episodes Of The Day" going! ^___^ And what better way to start it off than with the very first episode of Sonic SatAM - the Saturday Morning ABC Sonic series!

Todays episode is the pilot, titled "Heads Or Tails". On a raid in Robotropolis to retrieve metal cotter pins, Sonic runs into Muttski, his now roboticized pet dog and vows to return him to normal someday along with the rest of Mobotropolis. The Freedom Fighters also have to contend with a squadron of Buzzbombers carrying an orange defoliant [Agent Orange? Hmmmm...] intent on destroying the Great Forest and exposing Knothole. This is undoubtedly the debut episode as it was designed to attract the readers of the (then) new comic book, complete with the Pink Sally and a skinnier Rotor. In fact, All characters look as they did in the early comic days, even with Sally's original hairdo. The episode plays for the slapstick of the early comics all the way as well. Today's episode - "Heads Or Tails!" Be here for Episode 2 tomorrow! ^__^
