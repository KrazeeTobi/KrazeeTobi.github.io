Sonic HQ Fan Art

If you have fan art, please send it to hq_graphics@echidnoyle.org.

Here're some small rules:

  • Please do not send fan-made characters, unless they're with an official character. Why no fan-made characters you ask? Sorry to disappoint you, but this is a Sonic website. I hope that doesn't discourage you to send your renditions of official characters you've drawn.
  • Please send me your real name first, please, and then, if you want, your nickname. If you want to keep your real name unknown, then just send your full nickname, but I would prefer a real name.
  • Nothing over 300 KB, and in the event it does happen, I may have to reduce the quality of the image down to size.
  • Nothing inappropriate. (You know what I mean, you sick people, you..)
  • Jpegs or Gifs only please. If for whatever reasons you can't change it to a Jpeg or a Gif then I'll gladly change it for you, as long as you ask. Meanwhile, enjoy what we have here already!
