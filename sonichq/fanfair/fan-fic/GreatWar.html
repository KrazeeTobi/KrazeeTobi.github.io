What Do They Have Against Us? By Julie Fallner
Added 9-99
A semi-rhyming poem bashing Sega for some reason. The rhyming is awkward and the poem itself isn't that great... Some advice for fanfic writers and poets. If you're going to make a point, do that in editorials, not in your fanfic. If you're going to make a point, drive it home, instead of drilling it into people's heads, because nobody's going to accept it if you do. Rated G.
