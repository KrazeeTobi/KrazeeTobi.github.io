Who We Are - Staff, Contact, and Our Goal
Our Goal And Cornerstone

Every page needs a goal. Basically - what we strive for and what we want to do. Sonic HQ for a long time has had the stigmata of being a big boisterous page with a lot of "staffers" when in reality - thats not at all who we are or the sight we want. Though previous people have stressed that the page was competetive, under Vector and myself's hand, thats not really the way we want to be seen. True, the goal of some pages is to be competetive, but in all honesty we look at this as one of our personal webpages. This is a page we want to be able to put on a resume, a page where we can come in after school and tinker around with. A hub of sorts where we can be friendly, and others can be friendly to us. Rather than being seen as competetive, we want to be seen as friendly and creative. That is our goal.

With a page like this, you have to strive for professionalism, but you also have to keep in mind this is also our personal webpage. We design it so everyone can look at it, but we also design it so that when we finish making those graphics, or meeting those new people in our forum, or finishing a new page - we can be proud of it. After all - isn't that the goal of any page? What good is doing something, if you're not happy with it or can't take delight in it? If you don't, it becomes a chore.

People say quite often that the page isnt like how it was when Zifei (the previous webmaster who left to follow HIS creativity) had it or back when it was smaller. Yes, the bigger you get, the less personable a page can get. We hope to not lose this personability. And no Zifei isn't on the page anymore. This is Vector and WB's baby now. And while we may not be everything Zifei was when he did the page, we are who we are. And this page - we hope - will be a reflection of that. So don't come in here expecting the past, expect the present. We're not Zifei, we're Vector and WB and thats all we want to be. Nothing more, and nothing less.

And to end off our "mission statement" as we coined it, when Sonic HQ first started, the staff was made up of only a few people. Now the staff count have risen to more than ten. Just because there are multiple people on the page, doesn't make it easier to maintain. It actually makes it harder since so many people want to do so many things. So we try to come together to discuss what we want so no one steps on each others toes. Its not as easy as it sounds, but we think we do a good job of it nonetheless. The Sonic HQ staff is made of several parts, the html staff members, and the regular staff members and usual contributors. The html staff members do the actual update. What we look for is SERIOUS people who are creative and innovative, and willing to work in a group effort to do what we want to do - make the ultimate Sonic webpage. A true ultimate page is not just information and churning out news 24/7, but a true ultimate page does all that and provides a hub of creativity, ideas, responsibility, and most importantly - friendship. If you're interested in wanting to work on Sonic HQ for any reason, email us and tell us what you can do. Open positions are listed in the table to the right. The group is made up of a bunch of very compatible people and if you'd like to contact us, our emails are located below. There you will find our profiles, info about us, contacts and other stuffs...

Thanks for visiting, thanks for reading, and most of all thanks for asking. ^_^
