The Game Characters !!!

The Sonic series has become an all-star cast of characters - not just the blue hedgehog that we've all come to know and love. While it began with just Sonic and Dr. Robotnik, Tails soon entered the picture, then Amy Rose and Knuckles. Now, Yuji Naka and his staff at Sonic Team introduce us to new characters - Big The Cat and The Ganma Robot Series - and a whole new villian - Perfect Chaos! But for Sonic Adventure, the artwork is a departure from previous titles. It's got a raw, more urban look to it that fits the series and takes it to the next level...

When the game comes out in the states and we get it you can come to this page for a listing of different moves and other things you can learn about that characters.




Name: Sonic The Hedgehog
Species: Hedgehog
Color: Blue
Sex: Male
Eyes: Green
Age: 16
Ability: Speed, Sonic Spin
Weakness: Can't swim
Appearances in Games: All Sonic games
First Appearance In Games: Sonic The Hedgehog 1
Quote: "I'll never look back, I've got no regrets. 'Cause time doesn't wait for me...I choose to go my own way."

He's blue, he's fast, and he's back in Sonic Adventure protecting Earth this time around from the evil clutches of Dr. Robotnik (Dr. Eggman in Japan). Sonic's design has changed somewhat, with green eyes and a larger buckle on those sneakers of his. He's still as fast as ever, and you'll begin playing Sonic Adventure as Sonic (duh).




Name: Tails The Two-Tailed Fox
Species: Fox ( Kitsune )
Color: Golden Orange
Sex: Male
Eyes: Blue
Age: 10
Ability: Flying And Swimming
Weakness: None
Appearances in Games: All Sonic games except Sonic 1
First Appearance In Games: Sonic The Hedgehog 2 - Game Gear Version
Quote: "I wanna fly high so I can reach the highest of the heavens. Somebody will be waiting for me, so I have gotta fly higher!"

He's been Sonic's sidekick since the sequel to the original 16-Bit Sonic the Hedgehog. He's grown up a little bit more but he's still just a wide eyed kid running alongside of his big-brother Sonic! The kid who is exceptionally wise for his age has also returned with his bi-plane The Tornado - which has undergone a few changes of its own...




Name: Knuckles The Echidna
Species: Echidna
Color: Red
Sex: Male
Eyes: Black
Age: 15
Ability: Gliding, Digging, Climbing
Weakness: Heavy Sunlight
First Appearance In Games: Sonic The Hedgehog 3
Quote: "Born on an island in the heavens, the blood of my ancestors flows inside me. My duty is to guard the Master Emerald."

Ever since Sonic 3, Knuckles - Guardian Of The Floating Island - has been on the scene, once even starring in his own game (Knuckles Chaotix on the 32X). Knuckles comes back to help Sonic out, and he can still use his climbing and gliding moves around Sonic Adventure's 3D worlds.




Name: Amy Rose
Species: Hedgehog
Color: Pink
Sex: Female
Eyes: Green
Age: 10
Ability: Pico Pico Hammer Attack
Weakness: Limited Battle Ability
First Appearance In Games: Sonic The Hedgehog CD
Quote: "I'll always be there for you in the best and worst times. You can be my sweetest honey for all of eternity."

Amy Rose - the little tagalong wannabe girlfriend first appeared in Sonic CD and has been on the heels of Sonic following her little obsession ever since - much to Sonic's trouble....BUT this time sh'e grown up a little bit more as well and has gotten a complete makeover. Ditching the "cute little girl" look for something more her age, she still may not be the best in fighting ability, but at least now she may have a fighting chance in her age bracket...




Name: Big The Cat
Species: Cat
Color: Dark Blue/Purple
Sex: Male
Eyes: Black With Yellow Pupils
Age:Unknown
Ability: Fishing pole
Weakness: Slow
First Appearance In Games: Sonic The Hedgehog Adventure
Quote: "Keep striving for a new tomorrow. I've got nothing to worry me. Come on, let's just get up and go, go, go!"

The latest entry into the ever-growing Sonicverse, Big is just as his name implies. A large cat. A large cat living near the ruins who loves fishing, and carries a rod & reel with him everywhere he goes. His companion, Frog, one day happened to swallow a Chaos Emerald of all things, after which he mysteriously disappeared. Now Big is out to locate his little buddy, fishing pole in-hand.




Name: E-102 Gamma
Species: Badnik
Color: Multicolored
Sex: Male (???)
Eyes: Gray
Age: None
Ability: Laser Weapon
Weakness: None known at the moment
First Appearance In Games: Sonic The Hedgehog Adventure
Quote: "With a steel heart inside a steel body, I'm a high-tech assault machine. Watch out for the laser rifle on my right arm!"

The second of the E-100 series of Gamma robots, E-102 is fiercely loyal to Dr. Robotnik, but is so of his own free will. Codenamed "Gamma", the highly accurate shooting robot recognizes Sonic as his "sworn enemy"...at least he "thinks so"....




Name: Doctor Ivo Robotnik (Doctor Eggman - Japanese)
Species: Human
Sex: Male
Eyes: Jet Black With Red Pupils (Occasionally wearing goggles)
Age: Unknown
Ability: Super Genius I.Q Almost To The Point Of Insanity
Weakness: Completely Mad
Appearances in Games: All Sonic games
First Appearance In Games: Sonic The Hedgehog 1
Quote: "Am I crazy, or am I a genius? I've got all the technology in my hands. It's time for me to take over the world!"

He's back, he's big, and he's bad - and this time he's got a partner in crime and his hands on the Chaos Emeralds: A BAD combination if ever there was one! Not only that - he's got a new weapon to add to his arsenal - The Egg Carrier! He's set his sights on Planet Earth this time and his first stop in his mad conquest - Station Square....




Name: Chaos
Species: Living Chaos Energy In Water
Sex: Male
Eyes: Unknown
Age: Unknown
Ability: Able to maintain and manipulate water
Weakness: No Regards for life
First Appearance In Games: Sonic Adventure

The new guy on the block - Perfect Chaos IS the living embodiment of Chaos and the Chaos Emeralds itself in the form of a living liquid....Already having death on his track record with the destruction of an entire civilization, he won't hesitate to do it again and his partnership with the devilish doctor may just be something a little bit deeper in and of itself...




Name: Chao
Species: Living Chaos Energy In Water
Color: Blue
Sex: Mixed
Eyes: Blue
Age: None
Ability: Manipulates Water, Able to turn into different things
Weakness: None known at the moment
First Appearance In Games: Sonic The Hedgehog Adventure

Living right alongside the bustling human world, are the Chao. The Chao are mysterious creatures, with the ability to morph themselves into the physical shape of any other living animal. They also obtain these animals' special abilities, to create an infinite variety of shape, characters and movements. Are they somehow connected to Chaos? Its unknown at this time...




Name: The Gamma Robot Series (E-100 - E-105)
Species: Badnik
Color: Multicolored
Sex: Male And Female
Eyes: Multicolored
Age: None
Ability: Various guns and weapons
Weakness: Unknown at this time
First Appearance In Games: Sonic The Hedgehog Adventure

They are the death machines - The new lines of "Super Badniks" thats sole purpose is to do one thing - ERADICATE Sonic The Hedgehog at all costs! Armed to the teeth with an arsenal that could level Cleveland they have no qualms about killing the hedgehog on sight. It looks like Doc Robotnik has finally created the perfect war machines.....Little does he know that E-102 has a slight glitch....

Each of the characters will have its own strengths/weakness, and their own storylines that diverge from that of the others. Plus, levels played one way with one character will change when played with another. Should be very interesting playing through as all of them.
