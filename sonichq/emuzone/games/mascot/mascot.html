Super Mario Shutdown

First of all, before you go any further on this page lets get one thing straight - THIS IS NOT A FLAME PAGE AND WILL NOT BE TREATED AS SUCH ! Instead, I decided to keep this page at another angle more along the lines of comparing Sonic to other videogame and corporate mascots, be they well known or cheesy B-Movie creations like Krypto The Wonder Dog, and Beppo The Super Monkey. BLAH ON THEM ALL !!! :) All in all, this is gonna be the "fun" page! So in other words - DONT RUIN OUR FUN !!!!

So just what IS this page about anyway? Well - Along the lines of everyone who has ever played videogames, one question has passed everyone's lips: "If Sonic got into a fight with Mario, who would win?" Now you're probably thinking "A Sonic page thats doing this ??? Thier gonna be biased towards Sonic regardless!" Not so. Instead we decided to poke fun at the question and up the ante a little to this: "What if Sonic the Hedgehog and company got into a fight with EVERYBODY ?" Be they a videogame icon or a cultural icon we want to answer that question in a way not to insult the other characters, but instead look at it humorously! Can you imagine a fight between Doctor Robotnik and Team Rocket? Amy Rose VS Pac-Man? Batman VS Tails? The possibilities are endless...

So behold in all its glory...SONIC THE HEDGEHOG VERSUS THE WORLD !!! And Sonic HQ and videogaming in general will NEVER be the same again....

Stats: The first season Super Friends. This team has four people with no super powers (five if you count Aquaman), and a snickering dog in a cape. I'm talking about mischief more along the lines of, "Golly gee whiz Superman, what's in this radioactive monster containment room marked "DO NOT OPEN"? Let's open it and find out!"

Outcome Of The Fight: Do we even really NEED to tell you the outcome? Heck - Hello Kitty could beat The Superfriends to a pulp and not even break a sweat. Sonic and crew would win this hands down provided they doesnt die of complete boredom first.
