  The Sonic Jam 2 Petition
  Would you like to see SegaSonic the Hedgehog (Arcade), Knuckles' Chaotix, Sonic CD, and Sonic the Fighters released on a collection CD? Then go here and sign the petition! This petition is hosted by The SegaSonic Station.  
  The Sonic Championship Petition
  Another petition for Sonic the Fighters. This one is specifically for that game, instead of a 'collection' title.  
  Neo Green Hill Zone
  This is an excellent site with tons of gaming info.  
  Original Story Board Pictures
  An interesting collection of pics used by Sonic Team to plan Sonic 1. This page is part of The Super Sonic Speedway.  
  All About Sonic!
  This is the official Sonic page at sega.com. It includes some brief history and an interview with Yuji Naka.  
  Sonic Island
  Maintained by famed MIDI composer Koryan, this Japanese site has tons of fan art and provides an interesting look into the Sonic of the other side of the Pacific. Some unique tips can be found here, including maps of the S3&K Special Zones and charts that tell you how to control which bonus stage you get when you cross a star post in S3&K.
