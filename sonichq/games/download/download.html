Sonic 3D Blast Demo includes only the first act and the special stage. Also, it may not work on all computers (at least it doesn't on mine) due to its resolution. Oddly enough, I can still take screeenshots!

S&K Collection Demo lets you play the Angel Island Zone with any charater. The full version includes Sonic 3 & Knuckles, Sonic 3, Sonic & Knuckles, the Sonic Screen Saver, a HUGE archive of WAVs from previous games, and a HUGE collection of great-looking Sonic Art.

Sonic CD Demo includes the great help file (with a history of all the Sonic games), the first, ten-stage zone (time travel included) and two of the fantastic special stages. A Sonic classic! If you don't have the PC version already, you MUST buy it, but the demo should do for now.

Sonic R, the only 360 degree Sonic racing game, was a hit on the Saturn and it's even better on the PC! You can make your choice of weather (clear, rain or snow?), there is day/night/evening/dusk play, there are some fantastic new sounds, many Saturn bugs have been fixed, and now you can replay moments from your previous race! It's nothing like Sonic Drift 2 for game gear! The Demo, unfortunately, hasn't been made yet, but when it is I seriously recommend that you download it!
