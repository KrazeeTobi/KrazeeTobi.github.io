SegaSonic Beats All!
 

The year is 1990. After moderate success with the Master System, Service Games (Known as SEGA) has just released its first 16-bit platform. Unbeknownst to them at the time, this 16-bit machine, known as "Mega Drive" in Asia and Europe and "Genesis" in the Americas, was destined to become a household name, the top-selling system of all time, the eighth wonder of the world, and quite literally the console that changed the way the public viewed videogames forever. This entire change of fate was the sole responsibility of three men...and one blue hedgehog. Little did they know the speedy phenomenon they would unleash on the populace....

Welcome To The Games Gallery Page! Here you can find information on every Sonic related Game ever produced by Service Games - AKA Sega. Listed in order of the release dates, the exceptions being "Fighters Megamix", "Somari", "Christmas Nights", and "Sonic Schoolhouse", we can almost guarantee this games page is the most fulfilling and accurate Sonic games list on the net. In hopes of making this the ultimate SegaSonic compendium - each page will contain walkthroughs, regular codes, Game Genie and Game Shark codes, links to related music, Pro-Action Replay codes, secret tips, screenshots of all zones in all games, listing of comic adaptions, first appearances, and info on the lost or hard to find games like Sonic Arcade, Sonic X-Treme, the Sonic Crackers and Sonic 2 Beta. Heck, we even threw in the "Amazing Sonic" handheld game. ^_^ So sit back, relax, and in the words of Sonic R: Lets get ready to "Make it real..."
