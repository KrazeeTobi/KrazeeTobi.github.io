    General Info
    Intro
    Fighters MegaMix combines the revolutionary, hard hitting gameplay of Virtua Fighter 2 and Fighting Vipers. It includes all the characters, moves and arenas from the two most popular Sega Saturn Fighting titles, in addition to some special moves straight from VF3 arcade! Here’s a mega mix of gameplay info: There’s a total of 32 characters. Yep, that’s right - 32! - 11 from Fighting Vipers, 11 from Virtua Fighter 2, and 10 hidden characters (from Sega Saturn Arcade hits like V Cop2, Virtua Fighter Kids, Daytona - AND SONIC THE HEDEGEHOG CHAMPIONSHIP!!!!!). It’s up to you to unleash the hidden characters - you unlock 'em, you fight 'em. Choose from 32 arenas, each with a different style. Select between "Ruthless no boundary" or "Break-through caged in arenas". Fighters MegaMix has 8 fighting tracks, each containing 6 characters and a boss. Once beating the boss, he or she becomes selectable. Fighters MegaMix includes all the moves in Virtua Fighter 2 and Fighting Vipers plus new Virtua Fighter 3 moves which have been added to the VF2. All characters can perform the VF3 sidestep, that is, they can move in or out of the screen to avoid attacks and then counterattack their opponents.
