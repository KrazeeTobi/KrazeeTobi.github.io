    Tails' Music Maker
    Tails introduces children to the world of music. They\’ll learn about instruments, scales, rhythm and tempo through imaginative music activities. Match the musical sound with its instrument! Lasso notes to create a musical scale! Play your own tune on the music maker! An edutainment title for Pico. I guess you compose simple songs or something with Tails as the lead character. This wasn't released in Japan as far as I know.
