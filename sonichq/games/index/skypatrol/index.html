    General Info
    Info
    There was next to NO INFORMATION on this game...Thanks to Sonic fan Green Gibbon we were able to get some more information - The only time Sonic fans had ever heard anything about was is in Sonic Jam, and the screenshots show Tails that can carry rings in his hand while flying. All I know about this game is that it was only released in Japan for the Game Gear. Judging from the screenshots I've seen (in Sonic Jam), it looks very similar to Tails Adventure, only a tad more puzzle-based (which would, essentially, make it a skill game and not a platformer at all). The screens had Tails flying around in what appeared to look like Emerald Hill Zone (from Sonic 2 on Genesis). He was lugging around a Ring, and there were some Mario-ish blocks floating around in the air.
