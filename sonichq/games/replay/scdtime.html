Sonic CD Time Attack Challenge


Introduction and Updates:

If you have Sonic CD, please send me your times!

Since every category has openings, any valid time will make it onto the chart.


Include:

1. Any times you have that are good enough to go on the chart. Since there are currently openings in every category, that means any time that you have!
2. The name you want to use.
3. A color for your name. It can be a word (blue) or a hexadecimal code (0000FF).
4. If you want, an e-mail address or website that your name will link to.


Rules:

1. Please send in the times that are displayed on your Time Attack menu ONLY!
2. Don't send any times for regular levels, or times that you got while using a code.
3. Of course, you shouldn't send any times that you made up.
