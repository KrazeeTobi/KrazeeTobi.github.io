Fight 2 Metal Sonics at Once:

Activate Debug Mode. In the Sky Sanctuary Zone, progress to the last star post in the act. Don't go any further! Using Debug Mode, go up and you'll see the edge of the floor where you fight Metal Sonic for the last time in the act. Don't center the screen on it! Instead, move up and to the right.

You'll find the floor where you fought Metal Sonic when he copied Robotnik's attack from the Metropolis Zone. Center the screen on it, and both that Metal Sonic and the one from the end of the act will appear. This makes for a VERY tough fight.

Try to win without using Debug mode to revive yourself, and then try to win while taking only a certain number of hits. This is easiest with Sonic, thanks to his insta-shield. It is also possible, although difficult, to do this without Debug by flying to the platforms as Tails.
