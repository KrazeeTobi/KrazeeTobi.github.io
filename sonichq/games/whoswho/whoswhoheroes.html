Name: Sonic The Hedgehog
Species: Hedgehog
Color: Blue
Eyes: Green
Age: 16
Home: South Island
Likes: Dislikes: Ability: Speed, Sonic Spin, Insta-Shield, Homing Spin
Weakness: Can't swim
Appearances in Games: All Sonic games
First Appearance In Games: Sonic The Hedgehog 1
Quote: "I'll never look back, I've got no regrets. 'Cause time doesn't wait for me...I choose to go my own way."

Faster than a speeding bullet - it's "Sonic the Hedgehog" and when he isn't rearranging the landscape with "little brother" Tails, he can probably be found on his hideaway base of South Island. An excellent athlete, Sonic has been known to clock speeds faster than Mach 1 - not only that, but he's a highly skilled acrobat as well....His arsenal of moves include the Sonic Spin which can buzz through the thickest underground passageways, and his Double Jump in which he goes SO FAST he can literally "jump on air itself". The Spin Dash, Super Peel Out, and Strike Dash are three different ways of utilizing his techniques - not only that but he has learned to completely manipulate such items as flame shields - in a fireball dash attack, excel even higher on a "Super Double Jump" with an energy shield, and do a "Super sonic leap" with a Bubble Shield.

Although racing isn't particularly Sonic's interest, he does have a sleek red racecar he calls the Cyclone which he has used in two of the Chaos Grand Prix tournaments. When Sonic collects all seven Chaos Emeralds, he becomes Super Sonic!!! As Super Sonic, all of his natural abilities are enhanced and his speed is so incredible that he actually hovers a few feet above the ground rather than running on it! He is completely invincible to all attacks (except getting smashed or drowning). His color also changes from brilliant blue to shining yellow. However, he needs at least fifty Rings to utilize Chaos Emerald power, and he must keep a steady supply or he'll turn back to normal Sonic!!!




Name: Tails The Two-Tailed Fox
Species: Fox ( Kitsune )
Color: Golden Orange
Eyes: Blue
Age: 8
Home: South Island
Likes: Dislikes: Ability: Flying And Swimming
Weakness: None
Appearances in Games: All Sonic games except Sonic 1
First Appearance In Games: Sonic The Hedgehog 2 - Game Gear Version
Quote: "I wanna fly high so I can reach the highest of the heavens. Somebody will be waiting for me, so I have gotta fly higher!"

Miles Prower has never been just an ordinary kid...He was born with 2 tails, and for most of his life he had been made fun of and teased by the other kids about it. Not only that but he's an orphan as well - abandoned by his parentsthere was no one he felt he could turn to with his problems. That is - UNTIL Sonic showed up. Sonic helped Tails turn his "burden" into his greatest asset by teaching him to use his twin tails in flight. Propelling him to speeds ALMOST as fast as Sonic. By whirling his tails together like a helicopter, he can fly for short periods of time, as well as propel himself along the ground...fast enough to keep up with his spiny blue hero! Aside from his standard flying maneuver, Tails is also an expert swimmer, and has learned how to use his tails to attack with a special Propeller Dash move. Tails' goal in life is to be just like his hero and best friend, Sonic the Hedgehog...living the life of an adventurer. What became of his parents is unknown, but he and Sonic bonded like glue from the moment they first met....

Tails is very wise for his age and its shown in his hobbies - as he owns a red bi-plane, known as the "Tornado", which comes complete with attached rocket boosters and jet flight skill! With Tails piloting and Sonic riding on the wings, they can clear oceans and mountain ranges in half the time it would take them on foot. Tails also has a small racecar called the Whirlwind S7, which he used for both Chaos Grand Prix tournaments. It's max speed isn't very high, but it's light enough to "jump" into the air without much force. When Tails collects all seven Chaos Emeralds, he becomes Super Tails. As Super Tails, all of his natural abilities are enhanced. He is also invincible to all attacks (except getting smashed or drowning), and glows a bright orange-yellow color. Four Super Flicky birds come to his aid, destroying every Badnik in sight and clearing a path for him. However, he needs at least fifty Rings to utilize Chaos Emerald power, and he must keep a steady supply or he'll turn back to normal Tails.




Name: Knuckles The Echidna
Species: Echidna
Color: Red
Eyes: Black
Age: 15
Home: Floating Island
Likes: Dislikes: Ability: Gliding, Digging, Climbing
Weakness: Heavy Sunlight
First Appearance In Games: Sonic The Hedgehog 3
Quote: "Born on an island in the heavens, the blood of my ancestors flows inside me. My duty is to guard the Master Emerald."

Knuckles the Echidna is the last of a lost civilization of echidnas that flourished a over a century ago. This society mysteriously disappeared, and left many secrets and mystical powers in its wake - including that of: The Master Emerald - The LARGEST Chaos Emerald in existence. As the last living descendant of the civilization, it is Knuckles' sworn duty to protect Floating Island, all animals on it and the Master Emerald which keeps it afloat. When someone or something threatens the island, this feisty echidna's on the case! When the land is peaceful, however, Knuckles enjoys lounging around as much as Sonic does. He also knows all of the Floating Island's secret passageways and shortcuts, though, so even a little slacking off won't reduce his reaction time. Knuckles receives aid in his duty as guardian from the Chaotix: Mighty the Armadillo, Espio the Chameleon, Vector the Crocodile, and Charmy Bee. It was with their help that Knuckles was able to stop Dr. Robotnik's plan to steal the Power Emerald (which fuels Carnival Island) and save the six Chaos Rings that serve as its power source.

Occasionally - if time permits, he'll go down to the surface for a SMALL amount of fun. Knuckles has his own speedy little racecar known as the Tempest. This orange, jeep-like vehicle helped the echidna through the second Chaos Grand Prix tournament, and (like Tails' Whirlwind S7) is light enough to "jump" into the air with little force. When Knuckles collects all seven Chaos Emeralds, he becomes Super Knuckles. As Super Knuckles, all of his natural abilities are enhanced. He is also invincible to all attacks (except getting smashed and drowning), and glows a flashing pink color. However, he needs to have atleast fifty Rings to utilize Chaos Emerald power, and he must keep a steady supply or he'll turn back into normal Knuckles.




Name: Amy Rose
Species: Hedgehog
Color: Pink
Eyes: Green
Age: 8
Home: Unknown (possibly South Island)
Likes: Dislikes: Ability: Pico Pico Hammer Attack
Weakness: Limited Battle Ability
First Appearance In Games: Sonic The Hedgehog CD
Quote: "I'll always be there for you in the best and worst times. You can be my sweetest honey for all of eternity."

Amy Rose, the young hedgehog with a hopeless crush on Sonic! Sonic could do without her, but once she's got a hug in edgewise, there's no shaking her off...Little Amy is more than just an annoyance, though. She has links to the Little Planet, and can predict its future with tarot cards. She's also mastered the Heart Attack, a charm move that confuses its receiver into a mindless daze. She has few redeeming hand-to-hand combat qualities, but her Magical Hammer can easily pound a few braincells out of any opponents with just a light swing. It's this colorful, oversized mallet that she used to hold her own in the Sonic Championship fighting tournament. Her "Pico Pico Hammer Attack" also keeps her path clear in Sonic Adventure. Amy also has two small cars that she uses for travel. The Breeze is a small blue car that almost seems to have a mind and face of its own. It's the one she used for both of the Chaos Grand Prix tournaments. She's also got a red convertible, which she used in the World Grand Prix. It's lifeless and a little on the slow side, but it comes equipped with an automatic hover engine and a Turbo Boost! ....




Name: Mighty The Armadillo
Species: Armadillo
Color: Red, Yellow And Black
Eyes: Black
Age: 15
Home: Unknown (possibly South Island)
Likes: Dislikes: Ability: Super Strength, Kicking Off Walls
Weakness: Goes Into Situations Without Thinking
First Appearance In Games: SegaSonic The Hedgehog (Arcade)

Mighty The Armadillo is possibly the strongest of the Sonic cast seeing as how his strength is not that of a normal furry. He's very smart but often tends to jump into many situations without thinking.

Mighty may be most known as one of the memebers of the Chaotix but he also shares a bond with Sonic as it was him, another associate by the name of Ray The Squirrel, and Sonic that thwarted a plan of Robotnik years before Sonic had met Knuckles. He's an old friend of Knuckles, and joined up with the Chaotix when Robotnik threatened Carnival Island and the Chaos Rings.

He can jump up the steepest of walls with ease, and when he kicks-off, he can take even the most wary of Badniks by surprise. He can also accelerate top great speed by kicking off a wall and hitting the ground running.




Name: Vector The Crocodile
Species: Crocodile
Color: Green And Yellow
Eyes: Brown
Age: 16
Home: Unknown
Likes: Dislikes: Ability: Spin-Boost (Mid-Air Dash), Climbing, Sonic Headphone Blast
Weakness: Size
First Appearance In Games: Knuckles Chaotix

Vector may have a BIG mouth but that nothing compared to the size of his heart. Brash and brazen yet carefree and happy-go-lucky, Vector never lets a serious situation get in the way of a good time (or his favorite song). This cool croc is NEVER without his headset, even in the most dire of battles.

If there's anything Vector loves as much as music, it's eating and speeding. He loves the rush of running when he isn't jammin' with his friends or investigating the local food courts. It's not yet known exactly how Vector got hooked up with the Chaotix...

When Vector does a spin attack or spin dash, he rolls himself up into a ring shape. His Power Boost move works like a jump or spin-dash within a jump, giving this cool croc some extra height or some quick acceleration. He can also scale walls almost as fast as Knuckles.




Name: Espio The Chameleon
Species: Chameleon
Color: Usually Purple
Eyes: Black
Age: 17
Home: Unknown (possibly South Island or Isolated Island)
Likes: Dislikes: Ability: Running on Walls and Ceilings, Whirl Attack, Balance, Stealth, Tongue Whip Attack
Weakness: Claustrophobic, Goes Into Situations Without Thinking, Temper,
First Appearance In Games: Knuckles Chaotix

Espio is quick and strong, and loves freedom. He has an intense drive in everything he does - when he runs, he charges head-first and doesn't look back. His hot temper often leads him into rough situations, and his friend Charmy Bee sometimes has to remind him of his manners.

Espio values his freedom above all else, and holds a grudge against Robotnik for trapping him in the Combi Confiner several years ago. It was when Knuckles rescued him from the machine that Espio joined the Chaotix, and vowed to protect the island alongside Knuckles at all costs. Espio's lashing Tongue Attack can whip the hardest of Badniks into shape, and his whirling attack - a tornado version of the standard spin - can leave any opponents spinning their heads! His unique ability to run along walls and ceilings can get him out of any tight spots, and he can also change his color at will to blend into the scenery.




Name: Charmy the Bee
Species: Honey Bee
Color: Yellow And Black
Eyes: Brown
Age: 16
Home: Unknown
Likes: Dislikes: Ability: Flight, Bee Sting, Buzz Dash
Weakness: Size
First Appearance In Games: Knuckles Chaotix

Charmy Bee is the most sophisticated of the Chaotix...this guy knows his P's and Q's! Infact, he's often the one who has to keep Espio in line. Charmy flew to Floating Island several years ago in search of new types of flowers. It was there he met Knuckles and joined the Chaotix. Only he can handle tricky problems in such a cool and charming way, making him an invaluable member of the team. Charmy's small size and light weight make him a difficult target, which is compounded by his ability to fly out of any danger. Not only that - BUT his diminutive size makes for him being able to get into many places unnoticed. His unique Buzz-dash can tear right through the most powerful of Badniks with relative ease; and using Ring Power, this little honey bee is even strong enough to lift his friends into the air with him.




Name: Heavy (No picture available)
Species: Robotic Mechanic
Color: Silver
Eyes: None
Age: None
Home: Unknown
Likes: Unknown Dislikes: Unknown Ability: Invincibility, Strength
Weakness: Very Slow, Very Heavy, Almost No Jumping Ability
First Appearance In Games: Knuckles' Chaotix

Robotnik's former chief Mechanix, Heavy and Bomb defected during Robotnik's attempt to take over Carnival Island. Heavy is very calm, and sincerely wants to help - perhaps wanting to make up for his past assistance to Robotnik.

Although Heavy is very slow, he makes up for it with his incredible durability. It's literally impossible for him to take a hit or lose rings. He doesn't need to spin, either - he automatically damages enemies on contact. Unfortunately, he basically has to be pulled through the zones by someone faster, and it's hard for that person to stop once Heavy starts moving forward. By using linked rings, Heavy can act as a "ball and chain" - he'll slow down his companion, but the 2 of them will make a formidable opponent for Robotnik.




Name: Bomb (No picture available)
Species: Robotic Mechanic
Color: Black and Red
Eyes: None
Age: None
Home: Unknown
Likes: Unknown Dislikes: Unknown Ability: Exploding and Instant Repair
Weakness: Slower Than Most, Blows Up When Hit
First Appearance In Games: Knuckles' Chaotix

Robotnik's former chief Mechanix, Heavy and Bomb defected during Robotnik's attempt to take over Carnival Island. Bomb isn't really a fighter, but he is determined to help and puts his full effort into it. He is rather cautious, which is understandable, since he has a tendency to explode.

Although slow by Sonic character standards, Bomb is faster and much lighter than Heavy. He can explode, and then quickly repair himself. Unfortunately, this mechanism is set off if he takes a hit, and it can be very dangerous for any companion that is in range of the explosion.




Name: Bean The Dynamite Duck !!!!
Species: Mallard
Color: Green
Eyes: Black
Age: 15
Home: Unknown
Likes: Dislikes: Ability: Bombs and Explosives Genius
Weakness: The Brains Department.....
First Appearance In Games: Sonic Championship - Sonic The Fighters

When it comes to bombs and explosives Bean is a certified genius - but when it comes to brains....well lets just say he's in need of some SERIOUS therapy....But what do you expect when most of your life consists of getting blown up? Bean is a bomb-crazed duck with a certain wild streak that far too often gets him into more trouble than it's worth. He's VERY unpredictable and you never know what this bird might do next...Never one to fear the consequences of his actions, Bean jumped on the opportunity to enter the Sonic Championship tournament. When he's too weak to negotiate, his endless supply of explosives will do the talking for him...Bean however has his roots in many other Sega games and it's rumored that Bean is related to the two somewhat quieter ducks, Bin and Pin. Bin and Pin - along with a human comrade named Suoy, haven't been seen for years...(Bin, Pin, and Suoy first appeared in the 1988 Sega arcade game, Dynamite Dux.) What happened to them - along with thier relationship to Bean still remains a mystery.....




Name: Bark The Polarbear
Species: Polar Bear
Color: Cream White
Eyes: Black
Age: 16
Home: Aurora Icefield
Likes: Dislikes: Ability: Strength, Throws
Weakness: The Brains Department.....
First Appearance In Games: Sonic Championship - Sonic The Fighters

Bark is a large, furry polar bear who makes his home in the frozen regions of Mobius. After living most of his life in the Aurora Icefield, Bark has gained incredible stamina, especially to temperature. He spends all of his spare time lifting weights and sparring. He likes nothing more than a good fight. (He is particularly good at throws, and knows a great number of them.) When he heard about the Sonic Championship tournament, he figured it was the perfect place to test his skill and just have fun. Although he's not the most intelligent being on Mobius, when you're as strong as he is, brains come optional...
