Sonic HQ - The Games Page

Video games are a lot like socks, in that they tend to disappear quite frequently and it may take months for you to notice they're gone. Between the way game news comes and goes among Sonic fandom, forthcoming Sonic games or ideas are announced and already announced games are updated with great regularity. But if a developer cancels a game and you miss the relative news story, you may not realize the title's been dumped until you try to buy it two months later and catch odd looks from the retail staff at Toys R Us. That's what this section is dedicated to - the Sonic games and ideas that you may NEVER see or that have been held in the smaller corner of Sonic fandom...

You've seen the games. You've seen the way to play them and you've seen all the characters....or have you? Welcome to the "Hedgehog The Never Was" section of Sonic HQ ! For your eyes only we present the Sonic games that DIDN'T go into production, characters that never made it into the Sonic mythos, The Sonic 2 Beta, Sonic X-Treme, The elusive hacks that let you operate Super Mario in "Sonic 1", Sonic on the Super Nintendo, Sonic The Hedgehog 4: AKA Sonic Crackers, and the list goes on and on...

The Sonic Graveyard...

The Sonic Graveyard is a chronicle of, or rather a digitized resting place for, info on those Sonic games or aspects of Sonic gaming that were for one reason or another, nipped in the bud or otherwise. Also here are the hacks, images, and any other type of information we can provide you with! This feature Will strive to be extensive; however, it's not without omission. Don't doubt for a minute that there aren't games that were merely a gleam in developers' eyes - and even some 60 percent builds - that the press never caught wind of. So with that in mind, if you have info about a game that was canceled, or if you worked on a canceled game or have more info about the titles we've listed here, e-mail us, as we plan to hopefully update and expand this feature.
